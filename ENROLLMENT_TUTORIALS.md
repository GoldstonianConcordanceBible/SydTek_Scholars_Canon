# Parent Enrollment Tutorials
## SydTek Scholars Academy | Step-by-Step Guides

---

## How to Use This Document

This document contains two parallel tutorials:
- **Tutorial A:** For Delaware residents
- **Tutorial B:** For families in all other states (nationwide)

Both tutorials result in full legal enrollment in SydTek Scholars as a Delaware-registered private K–12 school.

---

## Tutorial A: Delaware Families

### Overview
You are enrolling your child in a Delaware-registered private school. Because SydTek Scholars is registered in your home state, the process is the simplest possible.

### Step 1: Visit the SydTek Scholars Enrollment Page
Go to: `sydtekscholars.com/enroll`
- Review the K–PhD pipeline
- Take the placement quiz (optional but recommended)
- Select your child's grade band: K–5 / 6–8 / 9–12

### Step 2: Complete the Enrollment Application
- Student legal name
- Date of birth
- Parent/guardian contact information
- Current grade level
- Prior school (if applicable)
- Learning goals and any special considerations

### Step 3: Pay Enrollment Fee
- Process through Shopify checkout at `sydtekscholars.com`
- Tuition tiers: Mirror (K–5) / Water (6–8) / Fire (9–12)
- Payment plans available

### Step 4: Receive Enrollment Confirmation
Within 48 hours you will receive:
- Official Enrollment Confirmation Letter (PDF)
- Student ID
- Onboarding guide
- Access credentials for the parent dashboard

### Step 5: Delaware DDOE Compliance (Automatic)
Because SydTek Scholars is registered with the DDOE, your child's enrollment is automatically compliant with Delaware compulsory attendance law. No additional filings required.

### Step 6: Withdraw from Prior School (if applicable)
If your child is currently enrolled in a Delaware public school:
- Contact your child's current school to notify them of withdrawal
- Request a copy of your child's records and transcript

### Step 7: Access the Parent Dashboard
Log in at `sydtekscholars.com/dashboard`
- View your child's K–PhD pipeline position
- Track accreditation progress
- Access curriculum materials
- Monitor microcredential progress

## Tutorial B: Nationwide Families (All Other States)

### Overview
You are enrolling your child in a Delaware-registered private school from outside Delaware. Your home state's compulsory attendance law is satisfied by enrollment in a registered out-of-state private school in virtually all states.

### Step 1: Check Your State Requirements
Reference the state table in [STATE_REGISTRATION.md](./STATE_REGISTRATION.md).
- Most states: keep enrollment confirmation on file — nothing else required
- Some states (GA, PA, VA, OR, WA, WI, NH, IA, NE): additional annual filing or testing required
- Contact SydTek Scholars admin if your state is listed as requiring additional steps

### Step 2: Complete the SydTek Scholars Enrollment Application
Go to: `sydtekscholars.com/enroll`
- Complete all fields including home state
- The system will flag any state-specific requirements based on your location

### Step 3: Withdraw from Prior School (if applicable)
- Request official withdrawal from your child's current school
- Obtain academic records, transcript, and any standardized test scores
- Send copies to: records@sydtekscholars.com

### Step 4: Pay Enrollment Fee
- Process through Shopify checkout
- You will receive your enrollment confirmation immediately upon payment

### Step 5: Receive Your State Compliance Package
Within 48 hours you receive:
- Official Enrollment Confirmation Letter (PDF) — use this to satisfy home state requirements
- State-specific compliance reference card
- Withdrawal letter template (if needed for your state)
- Curriculum overview for district compliance documentation (if required by your state)

### Step 6: File Any Required State Notices
Based on your state:
- **Georgia:** File Declaration of Intent at gadoe.org
- **Pennsylvania:** File affidavit with local superintendent
- **Virginia:** File annual notice of intent with local superintendent
- **Oregon:** Register with Oregon Dept. of Education
- **All others:** Keep enrollment confirmation on file — no further action

### Step 7: Access the Parent Dashboard
Log in at `sydtekscholars.com/dashboard`
- Full K–PhD pipeline visible
- Accreditation status live
- Curriculum access
- Microcredential tracker

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is SydTek Scholars accredited?**
A: SydTek Scholars is currently on the Cognia accreditation track (Year 2–3 target). Current accreditation status is visible in real time on the parent dashboard. We are legally registered and operating. Accreditation affects diploma recognition for college admissions — it does not affect the legal validity of enrollment or the quality of instruction.

**Q: Will my child's credits transfer to college?**
A: Students in grades 9–12 earn up to 12 microcredits toward SydTek University. Once Cognia accreditation is achieved, transcripts and diplomas are recognized by colleges nationwide. Microcredentials are stackable and do not expire.

**Q: Can my child stop at any grade and keep their credentials?**
A: Yes. Every certificate and credential earned is theirs regardless of whether they continue. No lock-in at any stage.

**Q: What if we move states?**
A: Your enrollment does not change. SydTek Scholars is a Delaware-registered institution — your home state does not affect your enrollment. You may need to file a new notice with your new state if required (reference the state table).

**Q: How does instruction work?**
A: Instruction is delivered through structured YouTube playlists, GitHub-documented courses, and reflection-based assessments. Parents and students access the curriculum through the Shopify-integrated dashboard.

**Q: Is this a homeschool program?**
A: No. SydTek Scholars is a registered private school, not a homeschool umbrella. This is an important distinction for state compliance and future accreditation.
