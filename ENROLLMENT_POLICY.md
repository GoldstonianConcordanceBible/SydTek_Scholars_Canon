ENROLLMENT PHILOSOPHY

SydTek Scholars seeks to provide
accessible, high-quality, faith-informed,
technology-enabled education that supports
student mastery, portfolio development,
Spanish language acquisition,
AI literacy, service learning,
and lifelong learning.
