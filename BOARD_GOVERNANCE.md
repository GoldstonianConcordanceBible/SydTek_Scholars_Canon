BOARD LIMITATIONS

The Board does not manage daily operations.
The Board does not replace academic staff.
The Board does not alter student records.
The Board does not bypass adopted policy.
The Board does not approve conflicts of interest
without disclosure and recusal.

⸻

CONFLICT OF INTEREST RULE

Any board member with a financial, family,
employment, vendor, or ownership interest
must disclose the conflict before discussion.
The conflicted member must recuse themselves
from voting.
