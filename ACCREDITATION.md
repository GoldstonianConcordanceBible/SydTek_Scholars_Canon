## Target Accreditor: Cognia

Cognia (formerly AdvancED / NCA / SACS) is the premier K–12 accreditor for online and nontraditional schools. Cognia accreditation grants:
- Nationwide diploma recognition
- College admissions transcript acceptance
- Military tuition assistance program eligibility
- Federal and state scholarship program eligibility
- Institutional credibility for K–PhD pipeline completion

## Cognia Eligibility Requirements

Before submitting an application, SydTek Scholars must demonstrate:

1. **Operational history:** 12+ consecutive months of student enrollment
2. **Governing board:** Functioning board of trustees with documented meetings and resolutions
3. **Financial soundness:** Audited or reviewed financial statements
4. **Curriculum documentation:** All courses have learning outcomes, scope & sequence, assessment rubrics
5. **Seat time documentation:** All courses meet minimum instructional hour requirements
6. **Student records:** FERPA-compliant recordkeeping system
7. **Mission and vision:** Documented institutional mission aligned with Cognia standards
8. **Faculty qualifications:** Instructors meet or exceed qualification standards for grade level
9. **Technology infrastructure:** Reliable delivery platform with documented uptime

## Educational Model Documentation

### Instructional Delivery
- **Primary:** YouTube-based structured playlists (video-first instruction)
- **Secondary:** GitHub-documented course materials (public, auditable)
- **Assessment:** Reflection-based written responses, structured assignments
- **Evidence:** Public video corpus, interaction data, progression logs

### Seat Time Model (per course)

20 videos × 10 minutes = 200 minutes instruction
20 reflections × 5 minutes = 100 minutes engagement
Total: 300 minutes (~5 hours) per course


Per academic year (30 courses):

30 courses × 5 hours = 150 instructional hours minimum
Additional project/assessment time: ~50 hours
Total estimated annual seat time: ~200 hours

## Board of Trustees

**Required:** Minimum 3 board members before Cognia eligibility application.

Board documentation maintained in:
`00-canon-governance/board/resolutions/`

Board must document:
- Meeting minutes (minimum quarterly)
- Policy adoption resolutions
- Financial oversight actions
- Accreditation progress reviews
