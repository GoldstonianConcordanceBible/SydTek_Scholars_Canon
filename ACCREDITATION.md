# Accreditation Alignment — SydTek Scholars

## Educational Model

SydTek Scholars operates as a structured K–12 program built on:

- Video-first instruction (YouTube playlists)
- Scripture-centered curriculum (GCB)
- Reflection-based assessment
- Public evidence of engagement

## Instructional Delivery

- Each course is delivered via structured playlists
- Lessons are sequential and cumulative
- Students engage through reflection and response

## Seat Time Model

- Average video length: 8–12 minutes
- 20 lessons per course (approximate)
- Reflection time per lesson: 3–5 minutes

Estimated total per course:
~4–6 instructional hours

## Assessment Model

- Comment-based reflection
- Written responses (optional structured assignments)
- Engagement tracking via public interaction

## Evidence Layer

- YouTube video corpus
- Playlist structure
- Comment engagement
- GitHub documentation

## Institutional Pathway

- SydTek Scholars (K–12 foundation)
- SydTek University (higher education extension)

## Positioning

SydTek Scholars is designed as a transparent, verifiable, and scalable educational system aligned with modern digital delivery and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO).
