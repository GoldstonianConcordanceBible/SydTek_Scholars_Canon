ISSUANCE PROCESS

Step 1
Student completes required evidence.
Step 2
Parent verification is completed.
Step 3
Faculty reviewer confirms learning evidence.
Step 4
Registrar approves credential.
Step 5
Credential is recorded in registry.
Step 6
Credential is linked to transcript or portfolio.
