PURPOSE

Establish the official student records policy
for SydTek Scholars, including enrollment records,
attendance records, academic records, portfolio records,
Spanish evidence, transcript records, credential records,
privacy protections, retention rules, and audit readiness.

RECORD ACCESS RULE

Student records may only be accessed by
authorized personnel with a legitimate
academic, administrative, compliance,
or parent-facing purpose.

⸻

RECORD UPDATE PROCESS

Step 1
Update request is submitted.
Step 2
Registrar reviews request.
Step 3
Supporting evidence is checked.
Step 4
Correction is made if approved.
Step 5
Change is logged.
Step 6
Parent or student is notified if needed.

RECORD AUDIT PROCESS

Quarterly
Registrar reviews active student records.
Annually
Chief Compliance Officer reviews records
for completeness, privacy, and accreditation evidence.
Before Graduation
Registrar and Chief Compliance Officer review
transcript and portfolio completeness.
