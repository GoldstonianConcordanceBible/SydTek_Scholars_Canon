PURPOSE

Establish the assessment system for SydTek Scholars
across Pre-K through Grade 12 using mastery checks,
portfolio evidence, parent verification, faculty review,
seat-time records, Spanish evidence, and annual progress review.

⸻

ASSESSMENT PHILOSOPHY

Fluency Not Exposure
Students are assessed by demonstrated learning,
not simple content exposure.

⸻

⸻

MINIMUM COMPLETION STANDARD

Students must complete at least 70% of required activities,
submit required portfolio evidence, complete parent verification,
and receive faculty review for grade advancement.

⸻
