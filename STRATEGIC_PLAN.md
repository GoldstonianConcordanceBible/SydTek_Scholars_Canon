PURPOSE

Establish the 1-year, 3-year, 5-year, and 10-year
strategic direction for SydTek Scholars and connect
mission, governance, curriculum, enrollment,
technology, staffing, finances, and accreditation.

⸻

STRATEGIC NORTH STAR

Build a lean, automated, accreditation-ready
K-PhD scholar pipeline using the
Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine and the principle
of Fluency Not Exposure.

⸻

Launch SydTek Scholars as a Delaware nonprofit
portfolio school and scholar network.

25-50 Scholars

Formation

⸻

YEAR 3 GOAL

Operate as a structured homeschool/private-school
support ecosystem with documented learning outcomes,
student records, transcripts, faculty review,
and accreditation evidence.
Target Enrollment:
150-250 Scholars

⸻

YEAR 5 GOAL

Achieve strong accreditation candidacy readiness
with full evidence folders, operational history,
student achievement data, governance documentation,
and continuous improvement cycles.
Target Enrollment:
500 Scholars

⸻

YEAR 10 GOAL

Operate as a recognized SydTek Scholars Network
with K-12 pathways, certificate pathways,
microcredential pathways, transfer pathways,
and future SydTek University alignment.
Target Enrollment:
2,500-5,000 Scholars
