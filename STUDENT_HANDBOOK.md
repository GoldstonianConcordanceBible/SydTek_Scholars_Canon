PURPOSE

Establish student expectations,
academic standards, graduation pathways,
portfolio requirements, Spanish requirements,
AI-use expectations, conduct expectations,
and accreditation-ready student policies.

⸻

MISSION

SydTek Scholars exists to develop students
who demonstrate fluency, not exposure,
through scholarship, service, stewardship,
leadership, wisdom, and lifelong learning.

⸻

STUDENT EXPECTATIONS

Students are expected to:
Complete assigned work.
Demonstrate academic growth.
Maintain portfolio evidence.
Participate in service learning.
Respect others.
Practice academic honesty.
Develop Spanish proficiency.
Use technology responsibly.
Follow community standards.

⸻

STUDENT RIGHTS

Students have the right to:
Access curriculum materials.
Receive academic feedback.
Receive fair evaluation.
Submit portfolio evidence.
Request academic assistance.
Participate in approved programs.
Appeal academic decisions through established procedures.

⸻

STUDENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Maintain learning records.
Complete required coursework.
Participate in portfolio development.
Complete service learning.
Maintain academic integrity.
Protect login credentials.
Use AI tools responsibly.
Follow digital citizenship expectations.

⸻

ACADEMIC INTEGRITY POLICY

Students may use AI tools for:
Research assistance
Idea generation
Draft improvement
Study support
Language practice
Students may not use AI tools for:
Submitting unreviewed AI-generated work
Fabricating sources
Completing assessments dishonestly
Misrepresenting authorship
Plagiarism

⸻

PORTFOLIO REQUIREMENTS

Student portfolios must include:
Reading Evidence
Writing Evidence
Math Evidence
Science Evidence
Social Studies Evidence
Covenant Evidence
Spanish Evidence
AI Literacy Evidence
Service Learning Evidence
Reflection Evidence
Annual Progress Evidence

⸻

SPANISH REQUIREMENTS

Spanish is required at every grade level.
Students must complete:
Vocabulary Activities
Reading Activities
Writing Activities
Speaking Activities
Portfolio Evidence

⸻

SERVICE LEARNING REQUIREMENTS

Students must participate in age-appropriate service.
Examples:
Family Service
Church Service
Community Service
Volunteer Projects
Leadership Activities

⸻

HIGH SCHOOL REQUIREMENTS

Grades 9-12 students must maintain:
Transcript Records
Credit Completion
Portfolio Evidence
Spanish Credit Progress
Service Learning Records
Capstone Evidence
Graduation Readiness Records

⸻

ATTENDANCE / PARTICIPATION

Students must maintain:
Seat-Time Records
Course Progress
Assignment Completion
Portfolio Progress
Parent Verification
Faculty Review Participation

⸻

DISCIPLINE PHILOSOPHY

Discipline is corrective rather than punitive.
Goals:
Restoration
Responsibility
Reflection
Growth
Accountability

⸻

DIGITAL CITIZENSHIP

Students must:
Protect personal information.
Respect others online.
Use technology responsibly.
Verify information.
Use AI ethically.
Report inappropriate behavior.
