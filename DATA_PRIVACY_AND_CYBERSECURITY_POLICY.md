Student information is a sacred trust.

parent, faculty, financial,
academic, and institutional records
through layered security controls,
role-based access management,
continuous monitoring,
and cybersecurity oversight.
Data protection is both an educational
and operational responsibility.

AI DATA PROTECTION POLICY

Private student data may not be entered
into unauthorized AI systems.
Student records must only be processed
through approved institutional systems.
Sensitive records must be redacted
before use in AI-assisted workflows.
Faculty remain responsible for all
AI-assisted outputs and reviews.
