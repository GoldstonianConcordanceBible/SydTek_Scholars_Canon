# Teacher Evaluation Rubric (v1.0)

## Dimensions (0–3)
1. Instruction clarity and alignment to outcomes
2. Assessment quality and use of rubrics
3. Feedback timeliness (SLA)
4. Classroom culture and communication
5. Integrity enforcement (citations, AI disclosure, honest work)
6. Safeguarding compliance
7. Privacy compliance and documentation discipline
