# Annual Renewal Workflow (v1.0)

## Purpose
Ensure adults renew commitments to safeguarding, privacy, and integrity annually.

## Workflow
1. Assign annual renewal form
2. Confirm training refresh completion
3. Review performance and any incidents
4. Reconfirm access needs (remove excess access)
5. Document renewal decision privately
