# Board Roles and Duties

## Board (collective)
The Board is responsible for:
- protecting mission and doctrine alignment
- approving major policies and revisions
- ensuring safeguarding, privacy, and financial integrity
- overseeing accreditation posture and claims accuracy
- maintaining an audit trail (minutes + resolutions)

## Chair
- sets agendas and facilitates meetings
- ensures decisions are documented
- confirms follow-through on actions

## Secretary
- maintains minutes and resolutions repository
- ensures records are stored and versioned properly

## Treasurer (optional)
- reviews budgets and finance reports
- ensures transparency and stewardship standards

## Policy Owners (non-board roles)
Policy Owners:
- maintain assigned documents
- propose changes through PRs
- keep revision histories accurate
