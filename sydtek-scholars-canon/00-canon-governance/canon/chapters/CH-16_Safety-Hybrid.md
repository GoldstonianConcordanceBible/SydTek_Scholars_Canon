# CH-16 — Safety & Hybrid Operations
**Status:** Draft (v0.1)  
**Purpose:** Define safety standards for online, in-person, and hybrid activities, including supervision rules and event approval controls.

---

## 1) Purpose
Safety is a non-negotiable stewardship obligation. This chapter defines:
- baseline safety expectations
- hybrid/in-person event approval controls
- supervision standards and ratios guidance
- incident response readiness (high-level)

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- any in-person meetups, co-ops, field trips, testing events
- hybrid events involving minors
- visitor and volunteer involvement
- emergency procedures and communications

## 3) Safety principles
1. Safety standards are written and trained.
2. No informal events without approval.
3. Supervision rules prevent isolation and reduce risk.
4. Incidents are documented and escalated appropriately.
5. Privacy and dignity are protected during incidents.

## 4) Hybrid event approval (control gate)
Minimum requirements before approval:
- purpose + agenda
- supervision plan and ratios
- location safety check
- emergency contacts plan (stored privately)
- consent forms
- transportation policy adherence (if applicable)

## 5) Supervision rules (public-safe summary)
- No 1-on-1 isolation.
- Clear adult-to-student ratios defined per event type.
- Background/clearance rules for volunteers per policy pack (later).
- Visitor sign-in and boundary rules per policy pack (later).
