# CH-15 — Faculty Standards
**Status:** Draft (v0.1)  
**Purpose:** Define faculty expectations: character, competence, training, planning standards, and evaluation.

---

## 1) Purpose
Faculty standards ensure:
- consistent instructional quality
- professional integrity
- safeguarding alignment
- reliable planning and assessment practices

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- hiring and onboarding
- training expectations
- instructional planning standards
- evaluation and improvement plans

## 3) Standards (non-negotiables)
1. Integrity and honesty (no deception in grading or reporting).
2. Planning discipline (outcomes, rubrics, artifacts).
3. Student care (support + consistency).
4. Privacy and data stewardship.
5. Safeguarding alignment and mandatory reporting adherence.

## 4) Training minimums (placeholder)
Training modules should include:
- safeguarding
- mandatory reporting procedures
- privacy and data handling
- academic integrity and AI integrity
- restorative discipline practices

## 5) Evaluation model (audit-friendly)
- observation rubric
- student outcomes review (aggregated)
- professionalism + documentation compliance
- improvement plan when needed
