# Transportation Policy (v1.0)

## Purpose
Ensure safe, documented transportation practices for any in-person events.

## Scope
Applies to any transportation arranged through the program.

## Policy (public-safe)
- Transportation plans are documented before events.
- Drivers must be approved adults.
- Student rosters remain private.
- Pick-up/drop-off procedures are clear and supervised.

## Required documentation (private)
- roster
- driver list/approval
- emergency contact access
- incident escalation plan
