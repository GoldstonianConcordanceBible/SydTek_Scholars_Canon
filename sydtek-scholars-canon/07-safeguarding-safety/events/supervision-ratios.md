# Supervision Ratios (v1.0)

## Purpose
Define minimum adult supervision standards for events.

## Policy (starter)
- K–2: 1 adult per 6 students (minimum)
- 3–5: 1 adult per 8 students
- 6–8: 1 adult per 10 students
- 9–12: 1 adult per 12 students

## Rules
- Minimum of 2 adults present for any group activity.
- No 1-on-1 isolation rule applies at all times.
- Volunteers follow volunteer policy and are never sole supervisors.
