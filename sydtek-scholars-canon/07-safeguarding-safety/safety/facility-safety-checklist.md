# Facility Safety Checklist (v1.0)

## Purpose
Maintain safe environments for any in-person or hybrid gathering.

## Use
- Before each event
- Monthly (if a consistent facility is used)

## Checklist
### Entry/Exit
- [ ] Clear entry/exit paths
- [ ] Emergency exits visible and accessible
- [ ] Attendance roster available (private)

### Supervision & boundaries
- [ ] Adult supervision ratio met (see events/supervision-ratios.md)
- [ ] No 1-on-1 isolation enforced
- [ ] Visitor policy followed

### Environment
- [ ] Restrooms monitored appropriately
- [ ] Trip hazards addressed
- [ ] First aid kit present
- [ ] Water and basic supplies present

### Emergency readiness
- [ ] Emergency contacts available (private)
- [ ] Staff know emergency procedure
- [ ] Communication plan tested (who calls whom)
