# Incident Response SOP (v1.0)

## Purpose
Respond to safety or safeguarding incidents quickly, consistently, and with privacy protection.

## Scope
Applies to:
- safety incidents at events
- online classroom safety issues
- safeguarding concerns (escalate immediately)

## Golden rules
- Protect students first.
- Do not share identifying details publicly.
- Document facts promptly.
- Escalate safeguarding concerns without delay.

## Steps (public-safe)
1. Ensure immediate safety
2. Notify event lead / safeguarding lead
3. Contain the situation (separate, supervise)
4. Document facts (private incident report)
5. Notify parent/guardian via approved channels
6. Determine corrective actions
7. After-action review + policy improvements if needed

## Documentation
- Full incident report is RESTRICTED (private storage).
- This repo stores only public-safe procedures and logs without identifiers.
