# Records Retention Standard (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define consistent retention expectations and prevent accidental loss or overexposure of sensitive records.

## 2) Scope
Applies to records referenced in this repo and stored privately.

## 3) Classification
- PUBLIC: may be stored in this repo
- RESTRICTED: private storage only
- REDACTED: may be published with identifiers removed

## 4) Retention table (starter)
| Record Type | Classification | Minimum Retention | Storage Location |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Attendance logs | RESTRICTED | TBD | Private records |
| Testing results | RESTRICTED | TBD | Private records |
| Immunization docs | RESTRICTED | TBD | Private records |
| Policies/templates | PUBLIC | Ongoing | This repo |
| Access logs | RESTRICTED | TBD | Private records |

**Note:** Fill “TBD” with your chosen retention windows after confirming your operational requirements.

## 5) Disposal rule
When retention expires:
- dispose securely
- log disposal action (private)
