# Attendance Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define a consistent, auditable approach to attendance documentation while protecting privacy.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all enrolled students and any instructional model (online, hybrid, or in-person components).

## 3) Policy
- Attendance is recorded on a consistent cadence.
- Records are stored securely and retained per retention standards.
- Public repo contains only templates and procedures; student-identifying logs remain private.

## 4) Procedure (public-safe)
1. Record attendance using the approved method and template structure.
2. Store student-identifying logs in the private records location.
3. Maintain an access log for sensitive records.
4. Produce aggregated totals when needed for internal monitoring (no identifiers).

## 5) Records & Retention
- Attendance logs: retain per `RECORDS-RETENTION_v1.0.md`
- Access logs: retain per `RECORDS-RETENTION_v1.0.md`
