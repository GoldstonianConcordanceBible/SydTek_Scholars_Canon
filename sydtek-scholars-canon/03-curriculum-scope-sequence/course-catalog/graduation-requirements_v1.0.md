# Graduation Requirements (v1.0)

## Purpose
Define credit expectations and ensure transcripts are honest and defensible.

## Credits (starter framework — adjust to your model)
- ELA: 4
- Math: 3–4
- Science: 3
- History/Social Studies: 3
- Health/PE: 1
- Bible Literacy: 1–2 (as offered)
- Electives: 4+
- Capstone: 1 (recommended)

## Credit definition
A credit is earned through:
- documented coursework completion
- mastery evidence (assessments + artifacts)
- integrity compliance (no falsified work)

## Evidence
- Each course must have a syllabus (from templates) and proof-of-learning artifacts (stored privately/redacted as needed).
