# Scope & Sequence — K–2 (v1.0)

## K–2 North Star
Build strong foundations:
- reading fluency and comprehension
- handwriting and clear writing
- number sense and core operations readiness
- observation and curiosity in science
- foundational Bible literacy and character formation
- gentle, age-appropriate Hebrew exposure (sounds, letters, simple words)

## Domains & outcomes (high-level)
### ELA (K–2)
- phonics + decoding
- sight words (as appropriate)
- read-aloud comprehension
- sentence writing → paragraph readiness
- speaking and listening norms

### Math (K–2)
- number sense (0–100)
- addition/subtraction fluency (grade-appropriate)
- patterns, shapes, measurement basics
- problem-solving explanation in simple language

### Science (K–2)
- observation and classification
- basic earth/space/life concepts
- simple experiments with reflection prompts

### History/Social Studies (K–2)
- family/community
- rules and responsibilities
- basic timelines and maps

### Bible Literacy / Formation (K–2)
- major Bible narratives (age-appropriate)
- memory work (short passages, themes)
- character habits: truth, kindness, discipline, service
- reflection: “meaning → action” (simple)

### Hebrew Thread (K–2)
- letter recognition and sounds
- simple vocabulary tied to Scripture literacy (age-appropriate)
- optional: basic transliteration exposure

## Proof-of-learning artifacts (minimum)
- reading log / fluency checks (private records; public-safe templates later)
- writing samples (2 per quarter)
- math mastery checks (regular)
- science observation journal (weekly)
- Bible literacy reflection artifact (monthly)
