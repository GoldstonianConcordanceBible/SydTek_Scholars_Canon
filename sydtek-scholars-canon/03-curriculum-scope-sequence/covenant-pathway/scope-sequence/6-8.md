# Scope & Sequence — Grades 6–8 (v1.0)

## 6–8 North Star
Build durable skills:
- analytical reading and structured writing
- pre-algebra → algebra readiness and reasoning
- scientific method + lab reasoning
- historical argumentation and evidence use
- Bible literacy: themes, doctrine basics, apologetics readiness (age-appropriate)
- Hebrew: grammar foundations + short passage reading (as applicable)

## Domains (high-level)
- ELA: essays, evidence-based writing, speaking/presentations
- Math: ratios, integers, equations, functions readiness
- Science: lab method, data interpretation, reasoning
- History: primary sources intro, argument + evidence
- Bible literacy/formation: interpretation basics, wisdom, disciplined habits
- Hebrew Thread: structured progression and proficiency checkpoints

## Proof-of-learning artifacts (minimum)
- argumentative writing pieces (2 per semester)
- math mastery checks with written reasoning
- science lab reports (redacted samples possible)
- history source analysis artifact (monthly)
- Bible literacy theme study artifact (quarterly)
