# Scope & Sequence — Grades 9–12 (v1.0)

## 9–12 North Star
Prepare for adulthood:
- strong writing, argument, and communication
- math/science mastery appropriate to chosen pathway
- historical literacy and civic reasoning
- Bible literacy and worldview integration with integrity
- Hebrew coursework/proficiency (as offered)
- capstone evidence of mastery + service

## Domains (high-level)
- ELA: literature + composition, research writing, speeches
- Math: algebra → geometry → algebra II (plus higher as offered)
- Science: biology/chemistry/physics (as offered), labs and reports
- History/Civics: US history, world history, civics/economics
- Bible literacy: structured study, ethics, worldview reasoning
- Hebrew: course sequence + proficiency milestones (as offered)
- Capstone: project demonstrating mastery and contribution

## Proof-of-learning artifacts (minimum)
- research writing + presentation
- lab reports and/or applied projects
- civic reasoning artifact (debate/essay)
- Bible literacy portfolio (selected artifacts)
- capstone portfolio (final evidence set)
