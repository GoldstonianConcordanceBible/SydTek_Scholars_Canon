# Scope & Sequence — Grades 3–5 (v1.0)

## 3–5 North Star
Strengthen mastery:
- reading comprehension and writing structure
- multiplication/division foundations and fluency
- scientific reasoning and simple lab practice
- expanding biblical literacy and worldview reasoning
- Hebrew literacy progression (vocabulary + reading practice)

## Domains (high-level)
- ELA: paragraphs → multi-paragraph, grammar basics, comprehension strategies
- Math: multiplication/division, fractions basics, multi-step problem solving
- Science: life/earth/physical science cycles, simple lab reports
- History: local → national → world intro, maps/timelines
- Bible literacy/formation: themes, wisdom, character + service projects
- Hebrew Thread: vocabulary growth, reading practice, basic structure awareness

## Proof-of-learning artifacts (minimum)
- writing portfolio (2 per quarter)
- math mastery checks + problem explanations
- science lab notebook excerpts (redacted examples possible)
- history timeline/map artifact (monthly)
- Bible literacy reflection + service artifact (quarterly)
