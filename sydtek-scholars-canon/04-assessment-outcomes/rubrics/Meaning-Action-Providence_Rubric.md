# Meaning → Action → Providence Rubric (v1.0)

## Purpose
Measure whether students can connect learning to meaning and responsible action, and reflect on outcomes.

## Scale (0–3)
### 1) Meaning (understanding)
0: unclear  
1: basic recall  
2: accurate explanation  
3: deep understanding and insight

### 2) Action (application)
0: none  
1: generic  
2: specific and realistic  
3: specific, realistic, and sustained

### 3) Reflection (learning from results)
0: none  
1: surface reflection  
2: identifies what worked/what didn’t  
3: proposes improvements and applies them
