# Writing Rubric (v1.0)

## Dimensions (0–3)
1. **Organization** (clear structure)
2. **Evidence/Support** (reasons and examples)
3. **Clarity** (sentence quality, word choice)
4. **Conventions** (grammar/spelling)
5. **Integrity** (citations, honest authorship, AI disclosure as required)
