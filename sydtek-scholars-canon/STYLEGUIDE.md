# Style Guide

## Document structure (required)
1. Purpose
2. Scope
3. Definitions (if needed)
4. Roles & Responsibilities
5. Policy / Standard
6. Procedures
7. Records & Retention
8. Revision History

## Naming
- Policies: `*_v1.0.md`
- Templates: `*_TEMPLATE.md` or `.csv`
- Logs: `*_LOG.csv`

## Tone
- Plain language, audit-friendly
- Avoid marketing language inside policies
