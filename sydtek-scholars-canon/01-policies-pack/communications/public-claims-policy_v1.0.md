# Public Claims Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Ensure all public statements are truthful, verifiable, and consistent.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any public-facing content.

## 3) Policy
- Any claim must be supportable by evidence.
- If evidence is not available, the claim must be removed or qualified.
- Corrections are made openly and logged.

## 4) Evidence mapping
- Claims about governance → link to governance docs/minutes/resolutions
- Claims about curriculum → link to scope/sequence + rubrics
- Claims about outcomes → link to reports (public-safe)
