# Accreditation Language Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Prevent misleading claims and ensure accuracy about accreditation status.

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- website, brochures, onboarding materials
- social media posts
- direct communications with families

## 3) Policy
- We do not claim accreditation unless formally granted and verifiable.
- We may describe readiness efforts and documentation posture truthfully.
- All accreditation statements must use approved language.

## 4) Approved language (starter)
- “We are building an audit-ready evidence library and readiness posture.”
- “We will only claim accreditation if and when formally granted.”

## 5) Prohibited language
- “We are accredited” (unless true and verifiable)
- “Accreditation pending” if it implies approval
- Any implied government endorsement
