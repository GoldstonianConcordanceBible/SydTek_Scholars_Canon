# Crisis Communications Protocol (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Provide clear communications steps during serious incidents while protecting privacy.

## 2) Scope
Applies to incidents involving:
- safety/safeguarding
- privacy/data exposure
- major operational disruptions

## 3) Rules
- Do not share identifying information about minors.
- Use a single designated spokesperson role.
- Communicate what is known, what is being done, and what comes next.
- Log actions and publish only public-safe updates.

## 4) Procedure (public-safe)
1. Confirm facts internally
2. Activate incident response lead(s)
3. Draft public-safe statement
4. Communicate to affected parties via secure channels
5. Post public-safe update if appropriate
6. After-action review and improvements logged
