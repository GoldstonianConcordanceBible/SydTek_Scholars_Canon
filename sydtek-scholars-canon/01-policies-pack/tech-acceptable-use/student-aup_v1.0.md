# Student Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Set clear expectations for safe, responsible technology use.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all students using program tools, accounts, or platforms.

## 3) Policy
Students will:
- use technology for learning purposes
- communicate respectfully
- protect logins and not share passwords
- not attempt to bypass controls or access restricted content
- not submit work dishonestly (see academic integrity)

## 4) Prohibited behavior
- harassment or bullying
- sharing private info (self or others)
- accessing or distributing inappropriate content
- cheating or misrepresenting work
- tampering with systems or accounts

## 5) Consequences
Violations trigger documented response steps aligned with discipline policy (later).
