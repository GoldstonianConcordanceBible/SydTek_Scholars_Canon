# Parent/Guardian Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define parent/guardian expectations for platforms, communications, and privacy.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any parent/guardian accessing platforms or communicating with staff.

## 3) Policy
Parents/guardians will:
- communicate respectfully
- protect student privacy
- follow documented complaint pathways
- support academic integrity expectations

## 4) Privacy rule
Do not post student-identifying information in public comments or social media related to school operations.
