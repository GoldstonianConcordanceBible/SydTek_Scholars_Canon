# Online Classroom Norms (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Make online learning safe, orderly, and respectful.

## 2) Norms
- Be on time and prepared
- Use respectful language
- Keep cameras/mics settings according to class expectations
- Do not record or share class content unless explicitly permitted
- No private messaging that violates boundaries or safeguarding rules

## 3) Moderation
Teachers may:
- remove disruptive participants
- document issues
- escalate safeguarding or privacy concerns immediately
