# Privacy Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Protect student and family privacy by defining data handling rules, limits, and accountability.

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- students, families, staff, volunteers, contractors
- all platforms/tools used for instruction and operations
- all records (public-safe, restricted, redacted)

## 3) Privacy principles (non-negotiables)
1. Collect only what is needed.
2. Limit access by role (least privilege).
3. Store sensitive data securely (private storage).
4. Do not publish student-identifying data in public repos.
5. Log access for sensitive records.
6. Respond quickly to privacy incidents.

## 4) Allowed disclosures (public-safe)
- policies, templates, procedures
- aggregated metrics (no identifiers)
- redacted examples (identifiers removed)

## 5) Prohibited disclosures
- student names, IDs, transcripts, test results, immunization docs
- incident details that identify minors
- private addresses or contact details without consent
