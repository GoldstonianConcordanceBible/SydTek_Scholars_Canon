# Admissions Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define fair, consistent admissions standards and a transparent enrollment workflow.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all applicants and enrollment cycles.

## 3) Policy
Admissions decisions are based on:
- program fit and family partnership readiness
- capacity to support student learning needs
- honesty and completeness of application information

Admissions decisions are not based on:
- manipulative marketing pressure
- undisclosed or inconsistent criteria

## 4) Workflow (high-level)
1. Inquiry → provide handbook + program expectations
2. Application → collect required info
3. Review → evaluate readiness and support needs
4. Decision → accept / waitlist / decline
5. Enrollment → agreement + onboarding

## 5) Records & Retention
Admissions records are RESTRICTED (private storage). Public repo contains only templates and procedures.
