# Scholarship Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Provide transparent, fair assistance criteria.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any scholarship/aid offered.

## 3) Policy
- Scholarships are awarded using documented criteria and a rubric.
- Decisions are recorded privately.
- Aggregate scholarship metrics may be published publicly (no identifiers).

## 4) Process
1. Application
2. Review using rubric
3. Decision (approve/deny/partial)
4. Notification
5. Renewal review (if applicable)
