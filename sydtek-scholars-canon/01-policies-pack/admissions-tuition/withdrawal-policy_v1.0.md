# Withdrawal Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define a consistent withdrawal process that protects families and record integrity.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any student leaving the program.

## 3) Policy
- Withdrawal requests must be submitted in writing.
- Records transfers follow the records transfer policy.
- Tuition refunds follow the tuition refund policy.

## 4) Procedure
1. Receive written notice
2. Confirm last day of participation
3. Update access permissions (revoke accounts as needed)
4. Prepare records per records transfer policy
5. Close out tuition/refund actions if applicable
