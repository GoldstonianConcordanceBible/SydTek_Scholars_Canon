# Academic Integrity Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Ensure student work truthfully represents student learning.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all assignments, projects, assessments, and portfolios.

## 3) Policy
- Work submitted must be honest and original unless collaboration is explicitly permitted.
- Sources must be acknowledged when used.
- Assistance must be disclosed when required (including AI).

## 4) Violations (examples)
- plagiarism
- unauthorized collaboration
- cheating on tests
- submitting AI-generated work as original when disclosure is required

## 5) Response
- Document the issue
- Apply consistent consequences
- Provide corrective instruction where appropriate
- Log patterns and improve prevention
