# AI Integrity Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Allow beneficial learning support while preventing dishonesty and protecting privacy.

## 2) Scope
Applies to students, staff, and families using AI tools in learning.

## 3) Policy
- AI may be used as a learning aid when allowed by the teacher/course rules.
- Students must follow assignment instructions about AI use.
- Students must not use AI to misrepresent mastery.
- Staff must not input RESTRICTED student data into unapproved AI tools.

## 4) Allowed (examples, when permitted)
- brainstorming outlines
- practice quizzes
- feedback on clarity (with student-authored draft)
- tutoring-style explanations

## 5) Not allowed (examples)
- generating final answers when the goal is mastery demonstration
- using AI during closed assessments
- submitting AI output as original student work without required disclosure
- placing private student data into unapproved tools

## 6) Disclosure
Use the standard disclosure line template when required.
