# Exam & Testing Security (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Protect fairness and credibility of assessments.

## 2) Scope
Applies to quizzes, exams, benchmark tests, and any controlled assessment.

## 3) Security practices (starter)
- clarify allowed materials
- define whether AI/tools are allowed
- use proctoring or supervision rules when needed
- separate practice from proof-of-mastery assessments
- document irregularities

## 4) Privacy
Do not publish identifiable testing records publicly.
