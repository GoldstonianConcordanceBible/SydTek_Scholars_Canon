# Plagiarism Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Prevent misrepresentation of authorship and teach proper citation habits.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all writing and project work.

## 3) Policy
- Students must cite sources.
- Copying without attribution is plagiarism.
- Paraphrasing without attribution is plagiarism.
- Using AI or other tools does not remove the need to cite sources.

## 4) Response
- Document
- Teach correction (how to cite)
- Apply consistent consequences per integrity policy
