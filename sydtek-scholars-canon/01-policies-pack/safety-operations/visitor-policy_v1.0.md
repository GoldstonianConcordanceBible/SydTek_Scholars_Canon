# Visitor Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Control adult access to student spaces and reduce safety risk.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any in-person location/event.

## 3) Policy
- Visitors must be approved and logged.
- Visitors remain supervised and within designated areas.
- No unsupervised student interaction.

## 4) Records
- Visitor sign-in log (template): `visitor-sign-in_TEMPLATE.csv`
