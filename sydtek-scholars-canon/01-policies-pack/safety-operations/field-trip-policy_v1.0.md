# Field Trip Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Ensure field trips are safe, supervised, and approved.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any off-site activity involving students.

## 3) Policy
- Field trips require approval, supervision plan, and consent.
- Transportation plans must be documented.
- Emergency readiness must be confirmed.

## 4) Required artifacts
- Approval checklist (recommended in later chunk)
- Consent form: `field-trip-consent_TEMPLATE.md`
