# Emergency Procedures (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Provide a simple, consistent emergency response approach.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all events and learning contexts.

## 3) Procedure (high-level)
1. Ensure immediate safety and supervision.
2. Contact emergency services when needed.
3. Notify designated leadership.
4. Document the incident (facts only).
5. Follow after-action review and corrective actions.

## 4) Privacy
Do not publish incident details with identifiers.
