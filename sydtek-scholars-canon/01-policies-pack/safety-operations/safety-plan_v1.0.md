# Safety Plan (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Ensure readiness for emergencies and safe operation of hybrid/in-person activities.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all events and any location used for learning activities.

## 3) Plan (baseline)
- Define emergency procedures for likely scenarios.
- Maintain emergency contacts privately.
- Conduct periodic safety reviews/audits.
- Require event approval documentation for hybrid/in-person activities.

## 4) Records & Retention
- Event approvals: retain per retention standard (public-safe copies allowed)
- Emergency contacts: RESTRICTED (private)
