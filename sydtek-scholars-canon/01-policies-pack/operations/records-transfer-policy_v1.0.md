# Records Transfer Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define how records requests and transfers are handled consistently and securely.

## 2) Scope
Applies to incoming/outgoing student record requests.

## 3) Policy
- Records are released only to authorized parties.
- Transfers are done via approved secure methods.
- A log is maintained privately.

## 4) Procedure (public-safe)
1. Receive request (use records request form)
2. Verify identity/authorization
3. Prepare requested records (minimum necessary)
4. Transfer securely
5. Log what was transferred and when
