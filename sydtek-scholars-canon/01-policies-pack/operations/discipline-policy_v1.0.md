# Discipline Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Maintain a safe, orderly learning environment using consistent, restorative, and documented practices.

## 2) Scope
Applies to student behavior across all environments.

## 3) Policy
- Discipline is corrective, not humiliating.
- Consequences are consistent and documented.
- Safety and safeguarding concerns are escalated immediately.
- Restorative steps are used when appropriate.

## 4) Procedure (high-level)
1. Document behavior concern
2. Apply restorative ladder (see `restorative-ladder.md`)
3. Create behavior contract if needed
4. Escalate severe or repeated issues
5. Log outcomes and follow-up
