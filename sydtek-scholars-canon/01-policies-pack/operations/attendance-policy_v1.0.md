# Attendance Policy (Operations) (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define participation expectations and how attendance ties to learning cadence.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all students.

## 3) Policy
- Families follow the published weekly cadence.
- Absences are communicated promptly.
- Patterns of non-participation trigger support escalation (see Student Support).

## 4) Procedure
1. Track participation using approved method (private logs)
2. Notify family if participation falls below expectations
3. Offer Tier 2 support interventions as needed
4. Escalate persistent patterns through documented pathway
