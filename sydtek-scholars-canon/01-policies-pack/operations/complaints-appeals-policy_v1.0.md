# Complaints & Appeals Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Provide a fair, documented pathway for concerns to be heard and resolved.

## 2) Scope
Applies to family and student complaints regarding academics, operations, discipline, or communications.

## 3) Policy
- Complaints are handled respectfully and documented.
- Complaints follow a defined escalation pathway.
- Retaliation is prohibited.
- Safeguarding concerns bypass normal complaint channels and follow safeguarding protocol.

## 4) Procedure
1. Submit complaint (use complaint form)
2. Acknowledge receipt within a defined timeframe (set your SLA)
3. Review against canon/policies
4. Provide written decision and rationale
5. Offer appeal pathway if applicable

## 5) Records & Retention
Complaint details are RESTRICTED (private). Public repo contains templates and procedures.
