# No 1-on-1 Isolation Rule (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Reduce risk by preventing isolated, unobserved adult-student interactions.

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- in-person meetings
- online meetings
- messaging and direct communications

## 3) Rule
- No adult may meet 1-on-1 with a student in an isolated environment.
- Approved safeguards (examples):
  - parent present
  - another approved adult present
  - visible/open setting
  - recorded session where permitted and governed (privacy rules apply)

## 4) Exceptions
Any exception must be pre-approved and documented with safeguards.
