# Safeguarding Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Protect students from harm through clear boundaries, supervision rules, training expectations, and documented response procedures.

## 2) Scope
Applies to:
- all staff, contractors, volunteers
- all learning environments (online/hybrid/in-person)
- all student interactions and communications

## 3) Roles & Responsibilities (public-safe)
- **Safeguarding Lead:** maintains policy, ensures training, manages incident process.
- **All Adults:** follow boundaries, report concerns, cooperate with investigations.
- **Board:** oversight of safeguarding standards and major changes.

## 4) Policy (non-negotiables)
1. Student safety is prioritized over convenience.
2. No 1-on-1 isolation (online or in-person) without approved safeguards.
3. Required reporting obligations are followed (see protocol).
4. Incidents and concerns are documented and escalated.
5. Privacy and dignity are protected.

## 5) Procedures (overview)
- Training completion is logged.
- Concerns are reported immediately through the documented pathway.
- Incidents are logged using the incident log template.
- Corrective actions are tracked and reviewed.

## 6) Records & Retention
- Training log (public-safe template): `training-log_TEMPLATE.csv`
- Incident log (public-safe template): `incident-log_TEMPLATE.csv`
- Incident reports (RESTRICTED): stored privately
