# Volunteer Policy (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Define expectations and boundaries for volunteers interacting with students.

## 2) Scope
Applies to any volunteer role across online/hybrid/in-person contexts.

## 3) Policy
- Volunteers follow safeguarding rules and supervision requirements.
- Volunteers do not handle private student records.
- Volunteers follow communication boundaries and reporting protocol.

## 4) Procedures (overview)
- Volunteer onboarding includes safeguarding training acknowledgment.
- Volunteers are supervised and never operate in isolation with students.
- Any concern triggers the mandatory reporting protocol.

## 5) Records & Retention
- Volunteer training records: retained privately as RESTRICTED.
