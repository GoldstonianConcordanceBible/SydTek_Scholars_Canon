# Mandatory Reporting Protocol (v1.0)

## 1) Purpose
Provide a clear, immediate-response pathway for safeguarding concerns.

## 2) Scope
Applies to all staff/volunteers and all environments.

## 3) Protocol (public-safe)
1. If a concern is observed or disclosed:
   - ensure immediate safety
   - notify the Safeguarding Lead immediately
2. Document the concern using the incident report form.
3. Follow required reporting obligations as applicable.
4. Do not investigate beyond your role; preserve information and escalate.

## 4) Communications rule
- Do not discuss details in public channels.
- Use approved internal/private channels and document appropriately.

## 5) Records & Retention
- Incident report (RESTRICTED) stored privately
- Incident log entry (public-safe index entry)
