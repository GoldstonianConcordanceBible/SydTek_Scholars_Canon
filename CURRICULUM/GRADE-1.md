LEARNING OUTCOMES

Bible / Covenant Studies

Student can explain:
God made families.
Families help us learn love.
Honor matters.
Obedience protects us.
Kindness builds peace.
Serving others honors God.

Reading

Read grade-level phonics words.
Read simple sentences.
Recognize common sight words.
Retell story events.
Identify characters and setting.
Read aloud with growing fluency.

Writing

Write complete simple sentences.
Use capital letters.
Use punctuation.
Write short responses.
Label drawings.
Write about family and community.

Mathematics

Count to 120.
Add within 20.
Subtract within 20.
Understand place value to tens.
Compare numbers.
Identify shapes.
Tell time to the hour and half-hour.
Measure using simple units.

Science

Identify living things.
Describe plants.
Describe animals.
Observe habitats.
Understand basic needs.
Observe weather patterns.
Record simple observations.

Social Studies

Identify family roles.
Identify community helpers.
Understand rules and responsibilities.
Describe neighborhoods.
Recognize basic maps.
Practice citizenship through kindness.

AI / Digital Literacy

Use technology with adult guidance.
Understand private information.
Practice safe device habits.
Know AI can answer questions.
Know AI is not always right.
Ask an adult before using online tools.

Art

Create drawings connected to stories.
Use color and shape.
Make family or community art.
Explain artwork orally.

Music

Sing simple songs.
Recognize rhythm.
Participate in call-and-response.
Connect music to memory and worship.

Health / Wellness

Practice hygiene.
Identify healthy foods.
Understand rest.
Name basic emotions.
Practice safe movement.
Ask for help when needed.

Service Learning

Help a family member.
Thank a community helper.
Make a kindness card.
Share with others.
Pray for family and community.
