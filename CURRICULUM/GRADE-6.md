LEARNING OUTCOMES

Bible / Covenant Studies

Student can explain:
Prophets called people back to God.
Truth requires courage.
Justice is part of covenant faithfulness.
Repentance means turning back toward what is right.
Worship and obedience belong together.
God warns, corrects, and restores His people.

Reading

Read grade-level literature and informational texts.
Identify theme, central idea, and supporting evidence.
Analyze character motivation.
Compare texts across genres.
Summarize complex passages.
Use context clues and reference tools.
Cite text evidence in written responses.

Writing

Write structured essays.
Write narrative and explanatory pieces.
Use claims, reasons, and evidence.
Write paragraphs with transitions.
Revise for clarity and organization.
Edit for grammar and mechanics.
Write prophetic witness and justice reflections.

Mathematics

Understand ratios and rates.
Use fractions, decimals, and percentages.
Solve multi-step word problems.
Understand negative numbers.
Work with expressions and equations.
Analyze data displays.
Understand area, surface area, and volume.
Apply mathematical reasoning to real situations.

Science

Understand Earth systems.
Describe ecosystems and interdependence.
Study energy flow.
Understand weather and climate foundations.
Study matter and physical properties.
Conduct simple investigations.
Use evidence to support scientific explanations.

Social Studies

Study ancient civilizations.
Understand geography and culture.
Analyze leadership, law, and religion in societies.
Read maps and timelines.
Compare civilizations.
Discuss justice, power, and responsibility.
Connect prophetic witness to social responsibility.

Spanish

Use greetings and introductions fluently.
Count 1-200 in Spanish.
Use family, school, community, justice, and worship vocabulary.
Read short Spanish paragraphs.
Answer oral questions in complete phrases.
Write simple Spanish sentences.
Use Spanish Bible vocabulary.
Describe routines, family, and community in Spanish.

AI / Digital Literacy

Use AI tools with adult guidance.
Create clearer prompts.
Compare AI responses with trusted sources.
Identify bias and misinformation at a basic level.
Protect private information.
Practice digital citizenship.
Use technology for research and presentation.
Explain why discernment matters online.

Art

Create art connected to truth, justice, or restoration.
Use design elements intentionally.
Create a visual project based on prophetic themes.
Explain artistic choices in writing or speech.

Music

Recognize rhythm, melody, tempo, and repetition.
Participate in group music.
Use Spanish vocabulary in songs.
Connect music to worship, memory, and justice.

Health / Wellness

Understand healthy routines.
Discuss stress, rest, and focus.
Identify emotions and self-control strategies.
Practice safe movement.
Discuss wise choices and responsibility.
Ask trusted adults for help.

Service Learning

Identify a need in family or community.
Complete a simple service project.
Write a reflection on justice and responsibility.
Encourage someone.
Pray for leaders, families, and communities.
Document service evidence.
