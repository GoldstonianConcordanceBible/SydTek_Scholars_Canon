LEARNING OUTCOMES

Bible / Covenant Studies

Student can explain:
God makes promises.
God keeps His promises.
Truth matters.
Faithfulness means keeping our word.
Waiting can be part of trusting God.
Families can remember God’s promises together.

Reading

Read grade-level passages.
Identify main idea.
Identify story sequence.
Retell beginning, middle, and end.
Recognize common spelling patterns.
Read aloud with fluency and expression.
Answer simple comprehension questions.

Writing

Write complete paragraphs.
Use topic sentences.
Use details.
Use punctuation correctly.
Write short narratives.
Write short explanations.
Write about promises, trust, family, and community.

Mathematics

Add within 100.
Subtract within 100.
Understand place value to hundreds.
Solve word problems.
Identify even and odd numbers.
Measure length.
Tell time to five minutes.
Understand basic money concepts.
Use simple charts and graphs.

Science

Observe earth materials.
Identify land, water, and sky.
Describe weather patterns.
Understand plants and animals.
Explain simple life cycles.
Record observations in words and pictures.
Ask simple scientific questions.

Social Studies

Understand community roles.
Read simple maps.
Identify landforms and locations.
Understand rules and responsibilities.
Explain basic history through stories.
Describe how communities keep promises.
Recognize symbols, traditions, and shared responsibilities.

Spanish

Use basic greetings.
Count 1-30 in Spanish.
Identify colors and shapes.
Use family vocabulary.
Use classroom vocabulary.
Answer simple questions.
Read simple Spanish words.
Sing simple Spanish songs.
Recognize Spanish Bible vocabulary.
Connect Spanish words to family, promise, and faith themes.

AI / Digital Literacy

Understand technology choices.
Know that online tools require adult guidance.
Identify safe and unsafe technology behavior.
Know AI can help with questions.
Know AI should not replace thinking.
Practice asking clear questions.
Understand that not every answer online is correct.

Art

Create drawings connected to stories.
Use color, line, and shape.
Create a promise-themed art project.
Explain artwork using complete sentences.

Music

Sing songs from memory.
Recognize rhythm and repetition.
Participate in call-and-response.
Sing simple Spanish vocabulary songs.
Connect music to worship and memory.

Health / Wellness

Practice hygiene.
Identify healthy foods.
Understand rest and exercise.
Name emotions.
Practice safe choices.
Ask trusted adults for help.

Service Learning

Keep a promise to help.
Thank someone who helps the family.
Make a promise card.
Serve a neighbor or family member.
Pray for someone in need.
