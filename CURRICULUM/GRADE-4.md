LEARNING OUTCOMES

Bible / Covenant Studies

Student can explain:
God leads His people.
God gives wisdom and instruction.
Leadership requires obedience.
Memory helps people remain faithful.
Courage is needed when following truth.
Worship reminds people who leads them.

Reading

Read grade-level literature and informational texts.
Identify theme and main idea.
Summarize chapters or sections.
Use context clues for vocabulary.
Compare characters and events.
Identify author purpose.
Support answers with text evidence.

Writing

Write multi-paragraph responses.
Use topic sentences and supporting evidence.
Write narratives with clear sequence.
Write explanatory paragraphs.
Revise and edit writing.
Use transition words.
Write leadership-themed reflections.

Mathematics

Multiply multi-digit numbers.
Divide with remainders.
Add and subtract fractions with like denominators.
Compare fractions.
Understand decimals.
Solve multi-step word problems.
Measure angles.
Interpret data and line plots.

Science

Describe matter and its properties.
Understand force and motion.
Explain energy transfer basics.
Study plant and animal structures.
Understand ecosystems.
Record observations and simple experiments.
Use evidence to explain patterns.

Social Studies

Understand leadership in communities.
Identify local, state, and national structures.
Read maps and timelines.
Compare cultures and regions.
Explain rights and responsibilities.
Describe how rules and laws guide communities.
Connect leadership to service.

Spanish

Use greetings and introductions with confidence.
Count 1-100 in Spanish.
Use family, school, community, and leadership vocabulary.
Read short Spanish sentences.
Answer simple questions orally.
Write simple Spanish words and phrases.
Use Bible vocabulary in Spanish.
Describe self, family, and community in Spanish.

AI / Digital Literacy

Use AI tools only with adult guidance.
Understand prompts and responses.
Check AI answers against trusted sources.
Protect private information.
Practice respectful digital communication.
Use technology for research and creation.
Identify misinformation at a basic level.

Art

Create art connected to leadership and memory.
Use color, line, shape, pattern, and texture.
Explain artistic choices.
Create portfolio-ready visual work.

Music

Sing songs connected to memory and worship.
Recognize rhythm, tempo, and melody.
Participate in group music.
Use Spanish vocabulary in songs.

Health / Wellness

Practice healthy routines.
Understand nutrition and exercise.
Identify emotions and self-control strategies.
Practice safe movement.
Understand rest and focus.
Ask trusted adults for help.

Service Learning

Serve a family or community need.
Create a leadership kindness project.
Help younger students or siblings.
Write a thank-you letter.
Pray for leaders and helpers.
Reflect on service and responsibility.
