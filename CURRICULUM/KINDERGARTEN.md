LEARNING OUTCOMES

Bible / Covenant Studies

Student can say:
God made me.
God made my family.
God made the world.
God wants me to love others.
God wants me to tell the truth.
God wants me to obey what is good.

Reading

Recognize uppercase letters.
Recognize lowercase letters.
Identify beginning sounds.
Read simple CVC words.
Recognize basic sight words.
Listen to stories and retell main events.

Writing

Write first name.
Trace letters.
Write uppercase letters.
Write lowercase letters.
Draw and label pictures.
Dictate or write simple sentences.

Mathematics

Count to 100.
Recognize numbers 0-20.
Compare more and less.
Identify shapes.
Sort objects.
Add within 10.
Subtract within 10.
Understand basic patterns.

Science

Observe weather.
Identify animals.
Identify plants.
Describe the five senses.
Understand day and night.
Observe living and nonliving things.

Social Studies

Identify family roles.
Identify community helpers.
Understand home and school rules.
Recognize basic maps.
Understand kindness and responsibility.
Describe personal history.

AI / Digital Literacy

Identify a computer.
Identify a tablet.
Use a mouse or touchscreen safely.
Understand screen-time rules.
Know that AI is a helper tool.
Ask an adult before using technology.

Art

Draw basic shapes.
Use color intentionally.
Create simple visual projects.
Explain artwork verbally.

Music

Sing simple songs.
Clap rhythm patterns.
Recognize loud and soft sounds.
Participate in group music activities.

Health / Wellness

Identify body parts.
Practice hygiene.
Understand rest.
Understand healthy food.
Practice safe movement.
Identify emotions.

Service Learning

Help clean a room.
Share with a sibling or friend.
Thank a helper.
Pray for someone.
Draw a kindness card.
