LEARNING OUTCOMES

Bible / Covenant Studies

Student can explain:
A covenant is a sacred promise.
God is faithful.
Covenant includes relationship and responsibility.
Obedience is connected to trust.
Families can live faithfully together.
God’s promises shape how people live.

Reading

Read grade-level stories and informational texts.
Identify main idea and supporting details.
Describe characters, setting, and plot.
Summarize short passages.
Use context clues.
Compare two texts on the same topic.
Read aloud with accuracy and expression.

Writing

Write organized paragraphs.
Use topic sentences and supporting details.
Write short essays.
Write narratives with beginning, middle, and end.
Use correct capitalization and punctuation.
Revise writing after feedback.
Write covenant-themed reflections.

Mathematics

Multiply within 100.
Divide within 100.
Understand place value to thousands.
Solve multi-step word problems.
Understand fractions as parts of a whole.
Measure time, length, mass, and volume.
Interpret simple graphs.
Understand area and perimeter foundations.

Science

Describe plant and animal life cycles.
Identify ecosystems.
Understand weather and climate basics.
Describe forces and motion.
Observe patterns in nature.
Record observations in a science journal.
Explain that systems have order and purpose.

Social Studies

Read maps and globes.
Identify communities and regions.
Understand civic responsibility.
Describe leaders and laws.
Compare past and present communities.
Explain how shared rules support community life.
Connect covenant responsibility to citizenship.

Spanish

Use greetings and introductions.
Count 1-50 in Spanish.
Use family, school, and community vocabulary.
Read simple Spanish sentences.
Answer simple oral questions.
Use Bible vocabulary in Spanish.
Sing or recite short Spanish memory passages.
Describe family and community in Spanish.

AI / Digital Literacy

Understand that AI tools respond to prompts.
Ask clear questions with adult guidance.
Know that AI answers must be checked.
Identify safe and unsafe online behavior.
Protect private information.
Use technology for learning, not distraction.
Practice respectful digital communication.

Art

Create art connected to covenant stories.
Use color, line, shape, and pattern.
Explain artistic choices.
Create portfolio-ready visual work.

Music

Sing songs connected to memory and worship.
Recognize rhythm, repetition, and melody.
Participate in group music.
Use Spanish vocabulary in songs.

Health / Wellness

Understand hygiene.
Identify healthy habits.
Understand emotions and self-control.
Practice safe movement.
Describe rest, nutrition, and exercise.
Ask trusted adults for help.

Service Learning

Serve a family member.
Help with a community task.
Create a kindness project.
Write a thank-you note.
Pray for someone.
Reflect on responsibility.
