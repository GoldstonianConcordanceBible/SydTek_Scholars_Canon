VENDOR REVIEW PROCESS

Step 1
Vendor need identified.
Step 2
President or department owner submits request.
Step 3
CFO reviews cost and budget impact.
Step 4
Chief Compliance Officer reviews privacy,
security, and policy impact.
Step 5
Board approval required if contract exceeds
approved spending authority.
Step 6
Vendor is added to vendor registry.

DATA AND SECURITY REVIEW

Vendors with access to student,
parent, financial, or institutional data
must be reviewed for privacy,
security, access control, and retention risk.

⸻

CONFLICT OF INTEREST RULE

Any board member, officer, employee,
or advisor with a financial interest
in a vendor or partner relationship
must disclose the relationship.
The conflicted party must not vote
on approval of the relationship.
