# Purpose

The SydTek Canon is the highest governing authority for the SydTek Alpha School repository.

It establishes the official institutional architecture, governance standards, educational philosophy, pricing framework, academic policies, and operational requirements for both:

- SydTek Scholars Alpha School (Kindergarten–Grade 12)
- SydTek University (Undergraduate–PhD)

Every document contained within this repository derives its authority from the SydTek Canon.

---

# Canonical Hierarchy

The SydTek Canon should always be interpreted in the following order of authority.

## Level I — Foundational Authority

1. Mission and Scope
2. Foundational Principles
3. Institutional Vision
4. K–PhD Educational Model

These documents define the institution itself.

No operational document may contradict these foundational principles.

# Amendment Authority

Changes to the SydTek Canon require:

- documented rationale;
- version increment;
- governance approval;
- change log entry;
- review for consistency with all canonical documents.

Operational documents may evolve more frequently than Canon documents.

---

# Repository Relationship

The SydTek Canon governs every folder within the repository.

No operational policy, pricing schedule, curriculum standard, AI policy, or accreditation document may supersede the SydTek Canon without an approved canonical amendment.

# Canonical Principle

Every institutional decision should be evaluated against one question:

> Does this strengthen the mission, educational quality, governance, transparency, student protection, and long-term sustainability of SydTek Alpha School?

If the answer is no, the proposed change should be reconsidered before adoption.
