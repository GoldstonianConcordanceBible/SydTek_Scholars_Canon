# Welcome

Welcome to the official repository for **SydTek Alpha School**, a unified educational ecosystem designed to serve learners from **Kindergarten through the Doctor of Philosophy (PhD)**.

SydTek Alpha School consists of two integrated academic divisions:

- **SydTek Scholars Alpha School** — Kindergarten through Grade 12
- **SydTek University** — Undergraduate, Graduate, Professional, and Doctoral Education

Together they form a continuous **K–PhD educational pathway** built upon competency-based learning, personalized assessment, responsible artificial intelligence, qualified human instruction, and transparent institutional governance.

---

# Mission

SydTek Alpha School exists to prepare learners for lifelong success through personalized, assessment-based education that values demonstrated mastery over seat time while maintaining academic rigor, ethical AI use, and human-centered instruction.

---

# Vision

To become a globally recognized K–PhD educational ecosystem where every learner progresses according to verified competency through an affordable, transparent, and continuously improving educational model.

---

# Core Principles

SydTek Alpha School is built upon five foundational principles:

1. **Assessment Before Instruction** — Every learner begins with a comprehensive academic assessment.

2. **Competency Before Seat Time** — Academic progress is based on demonstrated mastery rather than time spent in class.

3. **Artificial Intelligence Supports Learning** — AI enhances teaching, tutoring, and feedback while remaining under human oversight.

4. **Human Academic Responsibility** — Qualified educators retain responsibility for admissions, grading, competency verification, graduation, and academic quality.

5. **Transparent Assessment-Based Tuition** — Learners receive a personalized pathway and published tuition cap before enrollment.

# Educational Model

Every learner follows the same general pathway:

1. Initial Assessment
2. Prior Learning Review
3. Personalized Learning Plan
4. Competency Pathway
5. Educational Dataset Access
6. AI-Assisted Learning
7. Human Academic Review
8. Competency Verification
9. Credential Issuance (where legally authorized)

---

# Assessment-Based Tuition

SydTek Alpha School does not automatically charge identical tuition to every learner.

Instead, tuition is determined by:

- assessment results;
- demonstrated competency;
- educational pathway;
- instructional intensity;
- faculty involvement;
- research requirements;
- optional premium services.

Every learner receives a published maximum pathway cost before beginning instruction.

---

# Educational Datasets

Educational datasets are structured instructional repositories that may include:

- learning objectives;
- instructional resources;
- multimedia;
- projects;
- assessments;
- simulations;
- AI tutoring context;
- competency requirements;
- revision history;
- curriculum metadata.

Educational datasets are instructional assets.

They are **not** student education records.

Student records remain separately governed and protected under applicable law.
