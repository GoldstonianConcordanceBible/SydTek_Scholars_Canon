# Purpose

This document establishes the official pricing architecture for SydTek Alpha School.

Unlike traditional institutions that primarily charge tuition based upon semesters or credit hours, SydTek uses an **assessment-first, pathway-based pricing model**.

The objective is to:

- reduce unnecessary educational costs;
- eliminate payment for previously mastered material;
- improve educational affordability;
- maintain financial sustainability;
- support accreditation readiness;
- create predictable pricing for learners;
- align educational costs with demonstrated learning needs.

This pricing model is designed to balance learner affordability with institutional sustainability.

---

# Pricing Philosophy

Every learner begins with an Initial Assessment.

The assessment determines:

- current competency;
- educational gaps;
- required datasets;
- faculty involvement;
- anticipated pathway;
- estimated completion timeline.

Learners pay for the educational pathway they actually require—not an identical curriculum assigned to everyone.

---

# Pricing Principles

Pricing should be:

- transparent;
- published;
- predictable;
- equitable;
- competency aligned;
- evidence based;
- financially sustainable;
- accreditation compatible.

No hidden academic fees should be introduced after enrollment without proper disclosure.

---

# Assessment-Based Tuition Model

The pricing model consists of six components.

```text
Initial Assessment
        │
        ▼
Personalized Learning Pathway
        │
        ▼
Educational Dataset Assignment
        │
        ▼
Competency Verification
        │
        ▼
Optional Human Services
        │
        ▼
Credential Completion
```

Each component contributes to the learner's overall pathway price.

# K–12 Pricing

K–12 learners typically purchase annual educational pathways rather than individual datasets.

Recommended pricing ranges:

| Educational Level | Annual Pathway |
|---------------------|---------------:|
| Kindergarten | $900–1,500 |
| Elementary School | $1,200–2,000 |
| Middle School | $1,800–2,500 |
| High School | $2,200–3,500 |

These pathway prices include:

- educational datasets;
- baseline AI tutoring;
- competency assessments;
- learning analytics;
- progress reporting.

Optional services are priced separately.

Organizations may license SydTek resources.

# Pricing Transparency

Before enrollment every learner receives:

- assessment pricing;
- estimated pathway pricing;
- dataset requirements;
- competency blocks;
- optional services;
- refund information;
- reassessment pricing;
- maximum projected educational cost.

No learner should begin a pathway without understanding estimated financial obligations.

Pricing is designed to support:

- faculty compensation;
- AI infrastructure;
- educational dataset maintenance;
- student support;
- technology;
- compliance;

# Consumer Protection

Pricing materials must clearly distinguish:

- required charges;
- optional services;
- institutional licensing;
- subscriptions;
- assessments;
- refunds.

Marketing materials should accurately represent educational costs.

# Continuous Improvement

Pricing effectiveness is evaluated using:

- learner affordability;
- enrollment;
- retention;
- completion;
- institutional margin;
- faculty workload;
- AI utilization;
- learner satisfaction.

Adjustments should be evidence based.
