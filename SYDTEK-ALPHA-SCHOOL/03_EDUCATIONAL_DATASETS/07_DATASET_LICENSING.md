# Educational Dataset Licensing Standard

**Repository:** SydTek Alpha School  
**Section:** Educational Datasets  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Canonical Institutional Licensing Standard

---

# Purpose

The Educational Dataset Licensing Standard establishes how SydTek Alpha School licenses educational datasets to external organizations while protecting academic quality, institutional integrity, intellectual property, and learner outcomes.

Educational datasets represent one of SydTek's primary institutional assets.

Licensing allows schools, universities, employers, governments, nonprofit organizations, and workforce partners to use SydTek instructional infrastructure without transferring institutional governance or academic authority.

---

# Licensing Philosophy

Educational datasets are designed to be:

- reusable;
- scalable;
- modular;
- version controlled;
- continuously improved;
- AI compatible;
- institutionally governed.

Licensing enables educational innovation while maintaining centralized academic oversight.

Licensing instructional materials does not authorize another organization to represent itself as SydTek Alpha School.

---

# Guiding Principles

Educational dataset licensing should be:

- transparent;
- legally documented;
- academically responsible;
- version controlled;
- financially sustainable;
- quality assured;
- accreditation compatible.

Every license should clearly define rights, responsibilities, and limitations.

---

# Licensing Objectives

Educational dataset licensing supports:

- educational affordability;
- institutional partnerships;
- workforce development;
- lifelong learning;
- international education;
- AI-powered learning systems;
- curriculum modernization;
- institutional sustainability.

Licensing revenue supports continued dataset development and maintenance.

---

# What May Be Licensed

SydTek may license:

- lesson datasets;
- module datasets;
- unit datasets;
- complete course datasets;
- laboratory datasets;
- project datasets;
- competency frameworks;
- assessment frameworks;
- AI Context Datasets;
- curriculum maps;
- instructional templates;
- faculty guides.

Each licensed resource remains subject to version control.

---

# What Is Not Licensed Automatically

Licensing educational datasets does not automatically grant:

- institutional accreditation;
- degree-granting authority;
- teacher certification;
- university status;
- institutional branding rights;
- governance authority;
- access to student education records;
- use of restricted assessments.

These rights require separate legal authorization where applicable.

---

# License Categories

Recommended licensing categories include:

## Individual License

Supports one learner or educator.

Typical uses:

- independent study;
- tutoring;
- professional development.

---

## Classroom License

Supports one instructor teaching one class.

---

## School License

Supports institution-wide instructional use within one school.

---

## District License

Supports multiple schools operated by a single educational organization.

---

## University License

Supports higher education institutions.

May include:

- curriculum;
- AI tutoring;
- competency frameworks;
- faculty resources.

---

## Enterprise License

Supports:

- employers;
- workforce education;
- government agencies;
- nonprofit organizations.

---

## International License

Supports approved educational partners operating outside the institution's primary jurisdiction.

International licensing should address:

- localization;
- language;
- legal compliance;
- export controls where applicable.

---

# AI Licensing

AI-enabled educational datasets may be licensed separately.

Examples include:

- AI tutoring systems;
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) knowledge bases;
- institutional copilots;
- faculty assistants;
- learner assistants.

AI licenses should specify:

- approved models;
- update frequency;
- support obligations;
- permitted AI workflows.

---

# Version Control

Licensed datasets remain under SydTek version control.

Licensees should receive:

- version notifications;
- update documentation;
- change summaries;
- migration guidance.

License agreements should specify update expectations.

---

# Quality Assurance

Licensed datasets remain subject to institutional quality review.

Quality assurance may include:

- instructional review;
- accessibility audits;
- competency validation;
- AI evaluation;
- metadata verification.

Updated versions should maintain educational continuity.

---

# Faculty Governance

Faculty remain responsible for:

- instructional quality;
- competency alignment;
- assessment integrity;
- curriculum review.

Licensing does not transfer academic governance.

---

# Branding

Licensees should accurately distinguish between:

- SydTek-developed materials;
- locally developed materials;
- modified instructional content.

Brand usage should comply with institutional trademark policies.

---

# Intellectual Property

Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

- SydTek retains ownership of licensed datasets;
- licensees receive defined usage rights;
- unauthorized redistribution is prohibited;
- derivative works should follow license terms.

Ownership remains separate from licensing.

---

# Pricing Models

Licensing may use:

- annual subscriptions;
- seat licenses;
- institutional licenses;
- enterprise agreements;
- consortium pricing;
- negotiated contracts.

Pricing should consider:

- organization size;
- deployment scope;
- support requirements;
- update frequency.

---

# Support Services

Optional support may include:

- implementation consulting;
- faculty training;
- AI deployment;
- curriculum mapping;
- technical support;
- professional development.

Support services may be priced separately from licensing.

---

# Data Privacy

Educational dataset licensing should not include:

- student education records;
- personally identifiable learner information;
- confidential institutional records;
- protected assessment materials.

Only approved instructional resources should be licensed.

---

# Compliance

Licensees should comply with:

- applicable copyright law;
- accessibility requirements;
- privacy regulations;
- contractual obligations;
- institutional usage requirements.

Compliance responsibilities should be documented.

---

# License Termination

Licenses may terminate upon:

- expiration;
- material breach;
- nonpayment;
- misuse of intellectual property;
- unauthorized redistribution;
- violation of institutional policies.

Termination procedures should preserve legal and educational continuity.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Licensing effectiveness is evaluated using:

- partner satisfaction;
- renewal rates;
- instructional outcomes;
- faculty feedback;
- learner outcomes;
- support metrics.

Evidence informs future licensing models.

---

# Relationship to the SydTek Canon

The Educational Dataset Licensing Standard implements:

- Educational Dataset Standard;

- Institutional Licensing Framework;
- Financial Sustainability Principles.

Educational licensing supports institutional growth while preserving academic integrity.
