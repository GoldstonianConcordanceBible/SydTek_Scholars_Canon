# STS-HS-HIST-102 — U.S. History (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 10–11 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** None

## Description
U.S. history with civic reasoning, evidence evaluation, and structured writing.

## Outcomes
- Explain major periods and turning points.
- Analyze primary sources and historical claims.
- Write structured evidence-based essays.
- Identify civic themes and responsibilities.
- Maintain accuracy and avoid misinformation.

## Assessments
Summative: source-based essay + unit exams + presentation  
Portfolio: 2 primary source analyses + 1 essay

## Units
1) Foundations & Early Republic  
2) Expansion, Conflict, Reconstruction  
3) Industrialization to World Wars  
4) Modern America & Civic Themes
