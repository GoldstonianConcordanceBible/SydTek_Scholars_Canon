# STS-HS-SCI-102 — Chemistry (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 10–11 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** Algebra I (recommended)

## Description
Matter, atoms, reactions, and quantitative reasoning in chemistry.

## Outcomes
- Describe atomic structure and periodic trends.
- Balance and interpret chemical equations.
- Apply conservation and simple stoichiometry.
- Read data and explain results clearly.
- Follow safety and integrity rules in lab work.

## Assessments
Summative: unit tests + 2 lab/investigation reports  
Portfolio: 2 reports + 1 concept map

## Units
1) Matter & Atomic Structure  
2) Bonding & Compounds  
3) Reactions & Quantities  
4) Solutions & Applications
