# STS-HS-HEB-102 — Hebrew II (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 10–12 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Prereq:** Hebrew I

## Description
Build reading ability and grammar foundations with increased vocabulary and comprehension.

## Outcomes
- Read longer guided selections with improved fluency.
- Apply basic grammar patterns (intro level).
- Expand vocabulary and demonstrate mastery.
- Demonstrate comprehension through structured checks.
- Maintain disciplined weekly cadence.

## Assessments
Summative: proficiency checkpoints + comprehension tasks  
Portfolio: selected evidence samples (private/redacted)

## Units
1) Review & Fluency  
2) Grammar Foundations  
3) Reading & Comprehension  
4) Proficiency Review
