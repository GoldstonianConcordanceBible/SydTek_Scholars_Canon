# STS-HS-ELA-101 — English I (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 9  
**Credits:** 1.0  
**Term Length:** Full year  
**Prerequisites:** None

## Description
Foundations of literature study, writing clarity, grammar usage, and evidence-based responses.

## Outcomes
- Read and summarize literature accurately.
- Write organized paragraphs and short essays with evidence.
- Apply grammar and conventions in final drafts.
- Speak clearly in short presentations.
- Use citations responsibly for sources used.

## Materials
Texts: TBD (district/family choice)  
Supplies: notebook, writing tools

## Cadence
Instruction: 2 lessons/week + guided practice  
Practice: 3 short practice blocks/week  
Check-ins: weekly  
Feedback SLA: within 7 days

## Assessments
Formative: reading checks, writing drafts, vocabulary practice  
Summative: 2 essays/semester + 1 presentation/semester  
Portfolio: 4 polished writing samples/year
