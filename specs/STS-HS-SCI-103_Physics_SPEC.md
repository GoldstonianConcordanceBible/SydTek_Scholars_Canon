# STS-HS-SCI-103 — Physics (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 11–12 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** Algebra II (recommended)

## Description
Motion, forces, energy, waves, and basic electricity with modeling and problem-solving.

## Outcomes
- Solve motion and force problems with correct units.
- Explain energy and momentum concepts.
- Interpret graphs and experimental data.
- Build and justify simple models.
- Communicate reasoning step-by-step.

## Assessments
Summative: unit tests + 2 modeling tasks + investigation report  
Portfolio: 1 modeling portfolio + reflections

## Units
1) Motion & Forces  
2) Energy & Momentum  
3) Waves & Sound/Light (intro)  
4) Electricity (intro) + Review
