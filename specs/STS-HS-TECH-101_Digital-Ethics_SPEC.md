# STS-HS-TECH-101 — Digital Ethics (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 9–12 | **Credits:** 0.5–1.0 | **Prereq:** None

## Description
Responsible tech use: privacy, security habits, misinformation resistance, and respectful online conduct.

## Outcomes
- Explain privacy and data classification basics.
- Practice account safety and ethical online behavior.
- Evaluate information quality and avoid misinformation.
- Apply digital citizenship in real scenarios.
- Follow AUP rules consistently.

## Assessments
Summative: digital ethics case study + personal safety plan (public-safe)  
Portfolio: 2 case reflections

## Units
1) Privacy & Data Stewardship  
2) Security Habits  
3) Information Quality  
4) Applied Case Study
