# STS-HS-SCI-101 — Biology (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 9–10 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** None

## Description
Life science foundations: cells, genetics, ecosystems, and scientific reasoning.

## Outcomes
- Explain core biological systems and processes.
- Use scientific method vocabulary correctly.
- Interpret simple data and graphs.
- Write clear lab-style reflections (even if labs are simulated).
- Maintain integrity in data reporting.

## Assessments
Formative: quizzes, vocab checks, lab reflections  
Summative: unit exams + 2 investigation reports  
Portfolio: 2 investigation write-ups + 1 systems model

## Units
1) Cells & Systems  
2) Genetics & Inheritance  
3) Evolution & Diversity  
4) Ecology & Human Impact
