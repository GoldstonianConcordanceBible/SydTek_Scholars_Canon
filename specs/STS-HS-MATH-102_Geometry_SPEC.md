# STS-HS-MATH-102 — Geometry (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 10–11 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** Algebra I

## Description
Geometry with reasoning: shapes, proof thinking, coordinate geometry, and measurement.

## Outcomes
- Use geometric definitions and properties correctly.
- Solve problems involving congruence and similarity.
- Apply coordinate geometry and transformations.
- Explain reasoning with clear steps.
- Use measurements and formulas accurately.

## Assessments
Summative: unit tests + construction/performance tasks  
Portfolio: 2 reasoning write-ups + 1 applied design task

## Units
1) Foundations & Transformations  
2) Congruence & Proof Thinking  
3) Similarity & Trigonometry (intro)  
4) Coordinate & Measurement Applications
