# STS-HS-HIST-201 — Civics & Economics (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 11–12 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year or 1 semester (as offered) | **Prereq:** None

## Description
Civic literacy, governance basics, and economic reasoning with real-world applications.

## Outcomes
- Explain basic civics structures and civic responsibilities.
- Evaluate arguments and public claims using evidence.
- Understand basic economic concepts and tradeoffs.
- Build a simple budget or decision model.
- Communicate respectfully across disagreements.

## Assessments
Summative: civics argument essay + economics project  
Portfolio: 1 civics brief + 1 economics case study

## Units
1) Foundations of Civics  
2) Rights, Responsibilities, and Law  
3) Economics Basics & Tradeoffs  
4) Applied Project + Review
