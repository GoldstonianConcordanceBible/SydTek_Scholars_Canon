# STS-HS-ELA-104 — English IV (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 12 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** English III

## Description
Capstone-level reading and writing: synthesis, advanced argumentation, and college/career communication readiness.

## Outcomes
- Synthesize multiple sources into a coherent argument.
- Write clear, polished essays and practical documents.
- Lead and participate in structured discussions respectfully.
- Produce a final portfolio demonstrating growth and mastery.
- Apply integrity rules consistently (citations + AI disclosure).

## Assessments
Summative: synthesis essay, senior portfolio, final presentation  
Portfolio: curated “best work” set + reflection

## Units
1) Synthesis Foundations  
2) Advanced Argument  
3) Practical Writing (real-world)  
4) Senior Portfolio + Presentation
