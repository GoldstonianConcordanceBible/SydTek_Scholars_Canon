# STS-HS-BIB-101 — Biblical Literacy I (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 9–10 | **Credits:** 1.0 (or 0.5 if semester) | **Prereq:** None

## Description
Foundations of Bible literacy: narrative overview, major themes, interpretation basics, and disciplined study habits.

## Outcomes
- Summarize major biblical narratives and themes.
- Use basic study skills (context, genre, cross-references).
- Write clear reflections connecting meaning → action.
- Demonstrate integrity in interpretation (no misquoting).
- Communicate respectfully about beliefs.

## Assessments
Summative: theme study + reflection portfolio  
Portfolio: 4 reflections + 1 thematic map

## Units
1) Storyline Overview  
2) Themes & Wisdom  
3) Study Skills & Interpretation Basics  
4) Application & Service Reflection
