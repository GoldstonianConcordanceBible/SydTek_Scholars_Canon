# STS-HS-PE-101 — Health & PE (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 9–12 | **Credits:** 0.5–1.0 | **Prereq:** None

## Description
Health knowledge and responsible habits; physical activity goals documented privately.

## Outcomes
- Explain basic health concepts (sleep, nutrition basics, stress, safety).
- Practice goal-setting and reflection.
- Demonstrate responsible decision-making.
- Track activity privately and honestly.

## Assessments
Summative: health reflection portfolio + safety plan  
Portfolio: reflections (public-safe) + logs (private)

## Units
1) Foundations of Health  
2) Habits & Goals  
3) Safety & Wellbeing  
4) Review & Reflection
