# STS-HS-ELA-102 — English II (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 10 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** English I

## Description
Deepen literary analysis and argument writing with stronger evidence and clearer organization.

## Outcomes
- Analyze theme, character, and author craft with textual evidence.
- Write argumentative essays with claims and counterclaims.
- Revise writing for clarity, structure, and conventions.
- Deliver structured presentations with supporting sources.
- Maintain integrity in sources and AI disclosure where required.

## Assessments
Formative: annotation checks, thesis practice, draft cycles  
Summative: 2 argument essays/semester + 1 speech/semester  
Portfolio: 4 polished pieces/year

## Units
1) Analytical Reading  
2) Argument & Counterargument  
3) Craft, Rhetoric, and Style  
4) Research Writing (intro–mid)
