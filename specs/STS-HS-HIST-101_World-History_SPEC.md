# STS-HS-HIST-101 — World History (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 9–10 | **Credits:** 1.0 | **Term:** Full year | **Prereq:** None

## Description
Global history overview emphasizing timelines, cause/effect, and evidence use.

## Outcomes
- Build timelines and explain historical causation.
- Compare civilizations and systems fairly.
- Analyze basic primary/secondary sources.
- Write evidence-based responses.
- Discuss respectfully and accurately.

## Assessments
Summative: 2 DBQ-style tasks + unit exams  
Portfolio: 2 source analyses + 1 comparative essay

## Units
1) Foundations & Early Civilizations  
2) Empires & Exchange  
3) Revolutions & Modernity  
4) Global Conflicts & Contemporary World
