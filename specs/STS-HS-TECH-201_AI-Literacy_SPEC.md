# STS-HS-TECH-201 — AI Literacy (Spec) v1.0

**Grade Level:** 10–12 | **Credits:** 0.5–1.0 | **Prereq:** Digital Ethics (recommended)

## Description
Understand AI tools, limits, and integrity rules; learn to use AI as a learning support without dishonesty.

## Outcomes
- Explain what AI is (high-level) and its limits.
- Use AI for learning support appropriately (when permitted).
- Disclose AI assistance when required.
- Identify bias, hallucinations, and verification needs.
- Protect privacy by avoiding restricted data in tools.

## Assessments
Summative: AI-use portfolio + verification challenge  
Portfolio: 2 documented “AI used ethically” artifacts

## Units
1) AI Basics & Limits  
2) Verification & Truthfulness  
3) AI Integrity & Disclosure  
4) Applied Project
