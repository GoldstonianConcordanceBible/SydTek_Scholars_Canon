## Description
Advanced composition and rhetoric: reading complex texts, writing research-backed arguments, and communicating persuasively.

## Outcomes
- Evaluate arguments and evidence quality in texts.
- Write research-backed argumentative essays.
- Integrate quotations and citations properly.
- Present claims clearly with organized reasoning.
- Demonstrate disciplined revision habits.

## Assessments
Formative: source credibility checks, outlines, drafts  
Summative: research essay + rhetorical analysis + presentations  
Portfolio: 3 major polished works + reflections

## Units
1) Rhetoric & Reasoning  
2) Research & Source Evaluation  
3) Argument Across Genres  
4) Synthesis Writing
