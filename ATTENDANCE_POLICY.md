PURPOSE

Establish attendance requirements,
seat-time tracking procedures,
parent verification rules,
participation expectations,
intervention triggers,
and accreditation-ready attendance evidence.

⸻

ATTENDANCE MODEL

SydTek Scholars uses a flexible,
portfolio-supported attendance model.
Attendance is demonstrated through:
Seat-Time Logs
Course Progress
Assignment Completion
Portfolio Evidence
Parent Verification
Faculty Review
Dashboard Activity

⸻

ATTENDANCE STANDARD

Students must show consistent participation
in assigned learning activities.
Attendance is not limited to physical presence.
Attendance includes documented academic engagement.

⸻

⸻

ATTENDANCE RISK LEVELS

ON TRACK
Student is progressing normally.
WATCH
Student is missing some activity or evidence.
INTERVENTION
Student requires support plan.
INACTIVE
Student has not shown sufficient engagement.

⸻

INTERVENTION PROCESS

Step 1
Attendance concern identified.
Step 2
Parent notified.
Step 3
Registrar reviews records.
Step 4
Academic Director creates support plan.
Step 5
Student completes missing evidence.
Step 6
Progress reviewed.
