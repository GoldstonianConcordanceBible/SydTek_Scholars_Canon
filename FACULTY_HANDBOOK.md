FACULTY REVIEW PROCESS

Step 1
Student submits portfolio evidence.
Step 2
Parent verification is reviewed.
Step 3
Faculty reviewer checks learning outcomes.
Step 4
Faculty reviewer records feedback.
Step 5
Academic Director confirms advancement readiness.
Step 6
Registrar records completion.

⸻

AI USE POLICY FOR FACULTY

Faculty may use AI for:
Drafting feedback
Rubric support
Lesson planning
Portfolio organization
Progress summary drafting
Intervention planning
Faculty may not use AI to:
Replace human review
Fabricate student evidence
Assign grades without review
Approve work not submitted
Bypass academic judgment
