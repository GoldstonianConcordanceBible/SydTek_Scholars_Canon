## What SydTek Scholars Is

SydTek Scholars is a Delaware-registered, faith-based, minority-owned online private K–12 school operating under the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) ecosystem.

- **Registered:** Delaware Division of Non-Public Education (DDOE)
- **Curriculum:** GCB-aligned, Mirror → Water → Fire doctrinal framework
- **Delivery:** Video-first (YouTube playlists), GitHub-documented, reflection-based
- **Pipeline:** K–12 feeds directly into SydTek University (undergraduate through PhD)
- **Accreditation Target:** Cognia (Year 2–3)
- **Enrollment:** Open nationwide — all 50 states + international
