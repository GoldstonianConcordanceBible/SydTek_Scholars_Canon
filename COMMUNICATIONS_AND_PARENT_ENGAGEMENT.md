PARENT ENGAGEMENT MODEL

Parents are expected to:
Review student progress
Verify attendance
Support portfolio completion
Support Spanish practice
Monitor technology use
Respond to school communications
Participate in annual review

INTERVENTION COMMUNICATION

Intervention communication is required when:
Attendance is missing
Portfolio evidence is missing
Spanish evidence is missing
Course progress is low
Parent verification is missing
Faculty review raises concern
