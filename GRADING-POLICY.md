PURPOSE

Establish the grading policy for SydTek Scholars
across Pre-K through Grade 12 using mastery levels,
percent completion, portfolio review, faculty review,
Spanish evidence, and transcript-ready high school grades.

⸻

GRADING PHILOSOPHY

Fluency Not Exposure
Grades must represent demonstrated learning,
completed evidence, academic progress,
portfolio quality, and readiness for advancement.

⸻

SPANISH GRADING REQUIREMENT

Spanish must be included in every grade.
Spanish evidence may include:
Vocabulary Check
Oral Practice
Reading Practice
Writing Sample
Song / Memory Participation
Portfolio Artifact

⸻

GRADE ADVANCEMENT REQUIREMENTS

Complete 70% or more of required activities.
Submit required portfolio evidence.
Complete parent verification.
Complete faculty review.
Complete Spanish evidence.
Complete annual progress review.
Demonstrate readiness for next grade.

⸻

INCOMPLETE POLICY

If a student does not meet completion requirements,
the student receives:
Incomplete
The student must complete an intervention plan
before grade advancement or transcript credit.

⸻

FACULTY REVIEW POLICY

Faculty reviewers confirm:
Evidence is complete.
Learning outcomes are addressed.
Student work is grade appropriate.
Portfolio supports advancement.
Transcript credit is justified where applicable.
