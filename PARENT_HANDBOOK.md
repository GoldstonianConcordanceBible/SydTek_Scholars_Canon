PARENT ROLE

Parents and guardians are partners in the
SydTek Scholars learning model.
Parents help verify:
Attendance
Seat Time
Student Work
Portfolio Evidence
Service Learning
Spanish Practice
Technology Use
Annual Progress

⸻

PARENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Complete enrollment forms.
Maintain accurate student information.
Support student learning schedule.
Verify attendance and seat-time records.
Review student progress.
Help upload portfolio evidence.
Support Spanish practice.
Monitor AI and technology use.
Communicate concerns.
Participate in annual review.

⸻

ATTENDANCE VERIFICATION

Parents confirm:
Date of learning activity
Subject completed
Time spent
Evidence submitted
Student participation
Parent observation

⸻

PORTFOLIO SUPPORT

Parents assist with:
Saving student work
Uploading evidence
Labeling assignments
Tracking completion
Preparing annual review
Supporting student reflection

⸻

SPANISH SUPPORT

Parents support Spanish learning by helping students:
Practice vocabulary
Listen to Spanish songs
Complete oral recordings
Review Spanish worksheets
Submit Spanish portfolio evidence

⸻

AI AND TECHNOLOGY OVERSIGHT

Parents must supervise:
AI tool use
Internet access
Device time
Online research
Digital communication
Student privacy
Academic integrity

⸻

COMMUNICATION POLICY

Parents may contact:
Registrar
For records, enrollment, and transcripts
Academic Director
For curriculum, academic progress, and faculty review
Chief Compliance Officer
For policies, documentation, and compliance concerns
President
For institutional issues and major concerns

Attendance Summary
Course Progress
Portfolio Evidence
Spanish Evidence
Service Learning Evidence
Faculty Review
Parent Reflection
Grade Advancement Decision
