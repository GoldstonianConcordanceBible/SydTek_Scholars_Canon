# Product Page Payment Section

```text
Pay with $GCB

SydTek Scholars accepts $GCB as a supported tuition payment option for this product.

All tuition and product prices are denominated in U.S. Dollars. If you choose to pay with $GCB, the required amount is calculated using the live $GCB market price at the time your transaction is confirmed on-chain.

The transaction hash and blockchain timestamp serve as your official payment record.
```
