PRE-APPLICATION REVIEW PROCESS

Step 1
Chief Compliance Officer reviews evidence folders.
Step 2
President reviews institutional readiness.
Step 3
CFO reviews financial sustainability.
Step 4
Academic Director reviews curriculum and assessment.
Step 5
Registrar reviews student records and transcripts.
Step 6
Board reviews readiness summary.
Step 7
Board authorizes accreditation application.
