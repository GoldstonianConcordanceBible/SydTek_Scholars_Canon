# K–PhD Pipeline
## SydTek Scholars → SydTek University | Complete Institutional Pathway

---

## Overview

The SydTek ecosystem is the only faith-based, minority-owned, GCB-governed institution offering a documented, continuous K–PhD pathway on a single platform. Every stage feeds the next. No stage is required to proceed to the next. Every credential is owned by the student permanently.

---

## Pipeline Architecture


Governed throughout by: **Mirror → Water → Fire**

---

## Stage 1: K–5 | Foundation | Mirror Tier

**Ages:** 5–10
**Doctrine:** Mirror — Truth, accountability, biblical literacy
**Core Curriculum:**
- Biblical literacy (GCB 81-book canon introduction)
- Phonics and language arts
- Arithmetic foundations
- Introduction to Second Temple literature (age-appropriate)
- Character formation under GCB framework

**Assessment:** Portfolio-based, reflection journals, parent-verified progress logs
**Seat Time:** ~5 hours/week structured instruction
**Credential Earned:** Certificate of Biblical Foundation
**Feeds Into:** SydTek Scholars 6–8 (automatic progression)

---

## Stage 2: 6–8 | Formation | Water Tier

**Ages:** 11–13
**Doctrine:** Water — Refinement, correction, critical thinking
**Core Curriculum:**
- Second Temple literature and Dead Sea Scrolls overview
- World history and comparative civilizations
- Pre-algebra and mathematics
- Logic and argumentation
- Comparative theology introduction (GCB framework)
- Writing and rhetoric

**Assessment:** Written reflections, structured essays, oral defense (optional)
**Seat Time:** ~6 hours/week structured instruction
**Credential Earned:** Certificate of Scholarly Formation
**Feeds Into:** SydTek Scholars 9–12 (automatic progression)

---

## Stage 3: 9–12 | Fire Prep | Fire Tier

**Ages:** 14–18
**Doctrine:** Fire — Execution under pressure, legacy creation
**Core Curriculum:**
- Advanced theology (GCB full canon)
- AP-equivalent coursework (English, mathematics, sciences, social studies)
- Dual-enrollment microcredit courses (toward SydTek University)
- Blockchain and AI foundations
- Economics and financial literacy
- Research methods and academic writing
- Delaware and U.S. Constitution (required)
- Free enterprise system (required)

**Assessment:** Standardized-equivalent assessments, research papers, capstone project
**Seat Time:** ~7 hours/week structured instruction
**Credential Earned:** SydTek Scholars Diploma + up to 12 SydTek University Credits
**Feeds Into:** SydTek University Undergraduate (direct pathway with credit transfer)

---

## Stage 4: Undergraduate | SydTek University | Mirror Tier

**Duration:** 4 years (accelerated tracks available via prior microcredits)
**Programs:**
- Theology (GCB specialization)
- Artificial Intelligence
- Blockchain and DAO Governance
- Finance
- Education Administration

**Credit Recognition:** Up to 12 transfer credits from SydTek Scholars grades 9–12
**Credential Earned:** Bachelor's Degree
**Feeds Into:** SydTek University Graduate programs

---

## Stage 5: Graduate | SydTek University | Water Tier

**Duration:** 2 years
**Programs:**
- Master of Theology
- MBA (DAO Governance Track)
- Master of AI and Systems
- Master of Education Leadership

**Credential Earned:** Master's Degree
**Feeds Into:** SydTek University Doctoral programs

---

## Stage 6: Doctoral | SydTek University | Fire Tier

**Duration:** 3–5 years
**Programs:**
- PhD in Theology and Hermeneutics
- PhD in AI Governance
- PhD in Educational Leadership
- Doctor of Ministry (DMin)

**Dissertation:** Original research, publicly indexed, DOI-registered
**Credential Earned:** Doctoral Degree
**Legacy Output:** Published research, institutional contribution, GCB ecosystem advancement

## No Lock-In Policy

| Exit Point | Credential Kept | Credits Retained |
|---|---|---|
| After K–5 | Certificate of Biblical Foundation | Portfolio documentation |
| After 6–8 | Certificate of Scholarly Formation | Portfolio + progression record |
| After 9–12 | SydTek Scholars Diploma | Up to 12 university credits |
| After UG | Bachelor's Degree | All prior credits |
| After Graduate | Master's Degree | All prior credentials |
| After Doctoral | PhD | Full institutional record |

All credentials are:
- Documented in GitHub-indexed transcripts
- DOI-referenced where applicable
- Portable to any future institution (post-Cognia accreditation)
- Permanently owned by the student
