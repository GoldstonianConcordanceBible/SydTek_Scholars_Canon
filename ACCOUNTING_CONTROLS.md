DONATION CONTROLS

All donations must be recorded.
Restricted donations must be separately tracked.
Donor receipts must be issued.
Donation use must follow donor restrictions.
Board must review restricted fund balances quarterly.

⸻

VENDOR CONTROLS

Vendor name
Service provided
Contract amount
Approval record
Payment schedule
Conflict disclosure
Renewal date

⸻

CONFLICT OF INTEREST CONTROL

Any board member, officer, employee, or vendor
with a financial relationship to SydTek Scholars
must disclose the relationship in writing.
The conflicted person may not vote on approval
of the related transaction.
