TECHNOLOGY PRINCIPLE

Technology is a tool for learning.
Technology does not replace wisdom,
faculty review, parent oversight,
academic honesty, or student responsibility.

⸻

STUDENT TECHNOLOGY USE

Students may use technology for:
Coursework
Research
Portfolio Development
Spanish Practice
AI Literacy
Writing Support
Project Creation
Service Documentation

⸻

PROHIBITED TECHNOLOGY USE

Students may not use technology to:
Cheat
Plagiarize
Submit false work
Share private information
Harass others
Bypass parent supervision
Misrepresent AI-generated work as original

⸻

AI USE POLICY

AI may be used for:
Study support
Idea generation
Language practice
Draft improvement
Research planning
Portfolio organization
AI may not be used for:
Replacing student thinking
Fabricating sources
Completing assessments dishonestly
Submitting unreviewed AI output
Bypassing faculty review

⸻

PARENT OVERSIGHT

Parents must supervise:
Student device access
Internet use
AI tool use
Account passwords
Digital communication
Portfolio uploads
Privacy protection

⸻

DATA PRIVACY RULE

Student information must be protected.
Access must be limited to authorized users.
Records must not be shared publicly.
Private student data must not be entered
into unapproved AI tools.

⸻

Safety
