## Delaware Registration (Home State)

### Legal Basis
SydTek Scholars operates as a nonpublic private school registered with the Delaware Department of Education (DDOE) under Delaware Code Title 14.

### Day 0 Registration Steps

| Step | Action | Where | Cost | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Register as nonpublic school | education.delaware.gov → Families → K–12 → Homeschools and Private Schools | Free | Same day |
| 2 | Form LLC (SydTek Scholars Academy LLC) | corp.delaware.gov | ~$90 | 1–3 days |
| 3 | Appoint Registered Agent | Delaware resident or service | ~$100/yr | Week 1 |
| 4 | Obtain EIN | IRS.gov → EIN Online Assistant | Free | Instant |
| 5 | Open Business Bank Account | Any bank | $0–$25 | Week 1 |
| 6 | Report Annual Enrollment | DDOE portal, by Sept. 30 each year | Free | Annual |

### Delaware Ongoing Compliance

- Annual enrollment report to DDOE (by September 30)
- Maintain daily attendance records for all enrolled students
- Grades 8–12: Include U.S. Constitution, Delaware Constitution, and free enterprise system in curriculum
- Maintain written student records policy (FERPA-compliant)
- Report school closure + file transcripts (grades 9–12) with state superintendent if school ceases operation

### What Delaware Does NOT Require
- Curriculum approval
- State inspection before opening
- Approval of pedagogy or instructional model
- Accreditation to operate

## Nationwide Enrollment Compliance

SydTek Scholars is a Delaware-registered private school. Students from all 50 states may enroll. Each family is responsible for compliance with their home state's compulsory attendance laws. Enrollment in a registered out-of-state private school satisfies compulsory attendance in most states.
