# Transfer Credits & Microcredential Framework
## SydTek Scholars → SydTek University

---

## Core Principle

No credential earned at SydTek Scholars expires. Every certificate, credit, and microcredential is permanently documented in the student's GitHub-indexed transcript and is portable to SydTek University and (post-accreditation) to other institutions.

---

## Credit Transfer Table

| Stage | Credential | Credit Value | Transfer Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| K–5 | Certificate of Biblical Foundation | Portfolio documentation | SydTek University (supplemental) |
| 6–8 | Certificate of Scholarly Formation | Portfolio documentation | SydTek University (supplemental) |
| 9–12 Dual Enrollment | Theology 101 | 3 credits | SydTek University UG |
| 9–12 Dual Enrollment | AI Foundations | 3 credits | SydTek University UG |
| 9–12 Dual Enrollment | Financial Literacy | 3 credits | SydTek University UG |
| 9–12 Dual Enrollment | Research Methods | 3 credits | SydTek University UG |
| 9–12 Diploma | SydTek Scholars Diploma | Direct admission | SydTek University UG |
| UG | Bachelor's Degree | Transfer evaluated | Other institutions (post-accreditation) |

**Maximum credits transferable from grades 9–12 to SydTek University UG: 12**

---

## Microcredential Stack Logic

Microcredentials are designed to be stackable. Each one counts toward:
1. The next microcredential in the sequence
2. A certificate (stack of 3–4 microcredentials)
3. A degree (stack of certificates)

A student who never pursues a full degree still walks away with documented, verifiable competencies that are:
- Useful for employment
- Presentable to trade programs and community colleges
- Usable as evidence for professional certifications
- Indexed in the GCB ecosystem permanently

---

## Non-Degree Pathways

Students who complete K–12 without pursuing university still have:

| Completion | Value |
|---|---|
| Finished K–5 | Certificate of Biblical Foundation — lifelong |
| Finished 6–8 | Certificate of Scholarly Formation — lifelong |
| Finished 9–12 | Diploma + 12 credits — usable at any accepting institution |
| Earned microcredentials only | Stackable toward future enrollment when ready |

There is no time limit on stacking microcredentials toward a degree.

# Curriculum Framework
## SydTek Scholars Academy | GCB-Aligned Scope & Sequence

---

## Doctrinal Spine

All curriculum is governed by the Mirror → Water → Fire framework:

| Tier | Grades | Doctrine | Pedagogical Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Mirror | K–5 | Truth, accountability, foundation | Literacy, numeracy, biblical introduction |
| Water | 6–8 | Refinement, correction, depth | Critical thinking, comparative study, writing |
| Fire | 9–12 | Execution, legacy, application | Advanced coursework, dual enrollment, capstone |

---

## Core Subject Areas (All Grades)

Every grade band includes:
- **Theology:** GCB 81-book canon (age-appropriate depth)
- **English Language Arts:** Reading, writing, grammar, rhetoric
- **Mathematics:** Grade-appropriate arithmetic through advanced math
- **Science:** General science, earth science, biology, chemistry (by grade)
- **Social Studies:** World history, U.S. history, civics
- **Second Temple Literature:** Introduction (K–5), Survey (6–8), Advanced (9–12)

---

## Grades 9–12 Required Courses (Delaware Compliance)

Per Delaware Code Title 14 §4103, all nonpublic schools must provide instruction in:
- United States Constitution
- Delaware Constitution and government
- Free enterprise system

These are embedded in the 9–12 Social Studies and Economics curriculum.

---

## Delivery Format

Each course is structured as:

Course Unit
├── Playlist (YouTube — 20 videos, ~10 min each)
├── Reflection Prompts (per video)
├── Written Assignments (per unit)
├── Assessment (end of unit)
└── Progress Log (parent-verified)


All course structures are documented in:
`sydtek-scholars-canon/courses/`

---

## GCB Canon Integration

The 81-book Ethiopian Orthodox canon (the canonical foundation of the GCB) is integrated across all grade bands:

| Canon Section | K–5 | 6–8 | 9–12 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Torah (5 books) | Introduction | Survey | Advanced |
| Historical Books | Stories | Context | Analysis |
| Wisdom Literature | Proverbs focus | Psalms + Job | Full corpus |
| Prophets | Major prophets intro | Survey | Full prophetic corpus |
| New Testament | Gospels focus | Epistles intro | Full NT |
| Deuterocanon | Selected stories | Survey | Full deuterocanon |
| Ethiopian additions (Enoch, Jubilees, etc.) | — | Introduction | Advanced study |

---

## Assessment Philosophy

Assessment at SydTek Scholars is **fluency-based, not exposure-based.**

A student passes when they can:
- Articulate the concept in their own words
- Apply it to a new context
- Defend it under questioning (grades 6–12)

Grades are not the primary metric. Demonstrated fluency is.
